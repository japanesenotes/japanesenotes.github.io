# <bigfont> 奔</bigfont> - Run, Bustle 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 奔 | Run, Bustle | ほん, はし | [大](../radicals/大.md) (Big), [十](../radicals/十.md) (Cross), [廾](../radicals/廾.md) (Twenty),  | [Level 58](../levels/wk_level58.md) |

Previous kanji: [濁](濁.md) Next kanji: [肖](肖.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * You're walking down the street when you see <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> twenty</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> big</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> cross</span>es lined up in front of you. Crosses creep you out and this is way too many for you to keep walking so you decide to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> run</span> as fast as you can to get past them.
* Imagine trying to run down a street with twenty big crosses lining it. It's so scary that you keep running faster and faster as you go.
* You figure since you're already on the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> run</span> that you might as well <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hone</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ほん](https://jisho.org/search/ほん)</span>) your running skills while you make your way down this scary, cross lined street. 
* Imagine distracting yourself from the scary crosses by honing your running skills while you do it.
