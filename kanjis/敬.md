## Mnemonics
 * Writing a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> poem</span> in <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> winter</span> is the only way to get <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> respect</span> from other poets.
* Even better, write a poem about winter IN winter. Then they'll really respect you.
* Once you have their <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> respect</span> you get <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ca</span>ke (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [けい](https://jisho.org/search/けい)</span>)!
* The cake has the word RESPECT in fancy, cursive letters. Imagine eating that cake and taste the respect of your poet peers. Mm mm!
