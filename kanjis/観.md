# <bigfont> 観</bigfont> - View 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 観 | View | かん, み | [矢](../radicals/矢.md) (Arrow), [隹](../radicals/隹.md) (Turkey), [見](../radicals/見.md) (See),  | [Level 22](../levels/wk_level22.md) |

Previous kanji: [副](副.md) Next kanji: [値](値.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * There's an <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> arrow</span> in a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> turkey</span> that you can <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> see</span> in your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> view</span>. The turkey seems to be viewing you too, from afar.
* View the turkey as the turkey views you. View the arrow stuck in it too.
* As you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> view</span> the turkey, Genghis <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Khan</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かん](https://jisho.org/search/かん)</span>) suddenly comes into your view too. He towers over the injured turkey, leans down, and... pulls the arrow out!?
* As you view this strange scene, Genghis Khan turns and views you viewing him. He points two fingers to his eyes and then to you, and marches off into the woods. You also view the turkey running off in the other direction.
