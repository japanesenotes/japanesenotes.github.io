## Mnemonics
 * Whenever a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> turkey</span> comes in the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> door</span>, you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> employ</span> them.
* Imagine hundreds of turkeys walking in. The word has gotten out amongst the turkey community that if they walk through your door, you'll employ them.
* Although you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> employ</span> turkeys, when a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> 子</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こ](https://jisho.org/search/こ)</span>) walks through your door, you send them away.
* I will employ turkeys, but I will never employ children! you exclaim.
