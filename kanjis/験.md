## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> horse</span> or a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> squid</span>? That's the only question that is on your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> test</span>.
* This test is the most important test of your life, which is why it's confusing that there is only this one question: horse or squid?<br />If you choose horse jump to reading mnemonic A.<br />If you choose squid jump to reading mnemonic B.
* A. On the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> test</span>, you fill in the horse bubble. Immediately after, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Ken</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [けん](https://jisho.org/search/けん)</span>) the samurai stands up and yells, YOU FAIL THE TEST. THE CORRECT ANSWER IS 'KEN AND HIS FAITHFUL SQUID!' He stamps a big F on your test.<br />You have failed the test, go back to the meaning hint.
* B. On the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> test</span>, you fill in the squid bubble. Immediately after, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Ken</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [けん](https://jisho.org/search/けん)</span>) the samurai jumps up and yells, YOU PASS THE TEST! YOU CHOSE SQUID, WHO IS KEN'S BEST FRIEND. He stamps a big A on your test.<br />You have passed the test. Proceed to learn more kanji.
