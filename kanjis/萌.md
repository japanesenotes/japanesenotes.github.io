## Mnemonics
 * You have to put <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> flowers</span> in a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> bright</span> place if you want them to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sprout</span>. If they're left in the shade they'll just rot.
* Picture your flowers sprouting out of the ground in the bright spring sun.
* You want to grow these <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sprout</span>s in your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ho</span>me (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ほう](https://jisho.org/search/ほう)</span>), not outside. So you need to find the windows in your house that are the brightest.
* Take a walk around your home right now. Can you find the best place to sprout flowers? Remember, it has to be nice and bright! Not dark and damp. Don't put them in your closet.
