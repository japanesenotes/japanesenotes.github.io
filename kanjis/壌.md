## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> dirt</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> nurse</span> is someone who nurses dirt. When nursed, dirt becomes <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> soil</span>.
* Soil generally has more nutrients in it. It makes sense for dirt that's been nursed to become soil.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Soil</span> is a thing <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Jou</span>rm (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [じょう](https://jisho.org/search/じょう)</span>) the huge farmhand works on improving. If he makes the soil better, the farm will yield more crops.
* Imagine Jourm working with soil (not dirt!). Improving it by mixing in various ingredients. The soil this year is particularly lush, for some reason...
