## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> king</span> of the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> flowers</span> in the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> center</span> (of England, if you remember) has a special <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> crystal</span>. The king crystal.
* Picture the king holding this crystal aloft, giggling as the sun shines through it and sparkles.
* The king thinks everyone wants his <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> crystal</span> so he hides it in the giant <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> a</span>corn (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [えい](https://jisho.org/search/えい)</span>) in the center of the flowers in England. There's a tiny hole in the bottom of the acorn, and that's where this crystal stays hidden.
* Imagine going to England, to the giant acorn in the center of the flowers, and searching for the king's crystal. If you find it, you can probably sell it for a fortune! Or just make a really sweet necklace.
