## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> good</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> moon</span> is a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bright</span> moon. Not just in terms of light, but in mood as well. Like, gee wiz, what a bright, happy moon.
* Picture the good, good bright moon. Shining and shimmering and smiling down at us on Earth.
* It's so <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bright</span> that you can see all the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> roe</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ろう](https://jisho.org/search/ろう)</span>) in the sea glowing and reflecting the light like stars. And the tiny baby fishies inside the roe are smiling brightly back up at the moon!
* Picture this scene like it's a Disney cartoon. The good moon is smiling brightly down at the roe which are smiling right back. They might even start singing a song!
