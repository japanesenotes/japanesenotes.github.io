## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> tsunami</span> that's <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> blue</span> is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> pure</span>. Some tsunami are other colors, like brown or red because they aren't pure, they'll full of trash and poop and other horrible things. But a blue tsunami is completely pure!
* Picture this pure blue tsunami. You can see every crystal clear drop of water in it.
* Seeing this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> pure</span> blue tsunami inspires you to pull out your glowing blue <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sa</span>ber (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [せい](https://jisho.org/search/せい)</span>)! As the pure water comes toward you, you take one, long slash!
* SPLAASHH! The tsunami's pure water splits and it absorbed into your saber. Now it's pure too. And transparent!
