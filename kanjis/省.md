| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 省 | Conserve | しょう, せい, はぶ | [少](../radicals/少.md) (Few), [目](../radicals/目.md) (Eye),  | [Level 21](../levels/wk_level21.md) |

Previous kanji: [責](責.md) Next kanji: [増](増.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * You only have a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> few</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> eye</span>s so you need to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conserve</span> what's left.
* You only have like, three eyes left. If you conserve them .
* You're trying to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conserve</span> your eyes, when the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Shou</span>gun (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しょう](https://jisho.org/search/しょう)</span>) marches by. He stops, sees you, and demands you give him your remaining eyes! But sir, I'm conserving what little I have left!
* The Shougun stares down at you, eyes blazing, and you hand over all but one of your eyes. Now you really have to conserve, or you'll be blind. Thanks Shougun. I bet he has plenty of eyes, he didn't need yours.
