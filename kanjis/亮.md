# <bigfont> 亮</bigfont> - Clear

## Mnemonics
 * There is a special type of meditation where you put a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> lid</span> on your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span> and touch your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> forehead</span> down to your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> legs</span>. If you can do this pose, everything in life will become completely <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> clear</span>. Everything will clear out of your head too. You should try it.
* Imagine this pose, imagine everything becoming clear. Clear your mind. Clear your head.
* Your mind is as <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> clear</span> as a sparkling clear pond. On this pond you see yourself in a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> row</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [りょう](https://jisho.org/search/りょう)</span>) boat. A single row boat on the clear pond that is now your mind.
* Picture the row boat on the clear pond in your mind. Watch the clear water flow over the row boat's oars.
