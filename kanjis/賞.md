# <bigfont> 賞</bigfont> - Prize 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 賞 | Prize | しょう | [口](../radicals/口.md) (Mouth), [貝](../radicals/貝.md) (Shellfish),  | [Level 13](../levels/wk_level13.md) |

Previous kanji: [課](課.md) Next kanji: [輪](輪.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * Shoot a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> shellfish</span> that's in the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span> of a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> Viking</span> at the fair and you'll win a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> prize</span>. The game is to shoot the shellfish in the Viking's mouth and knock it over.
* Imagine looking at a big Viking with a shellfish in its mouth. You want to knock it over. You want this prize.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> prize</span> you're working so hard to win is lunch with the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Shou</span>gun (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しょう](https://jisho.org/search/しょう)</span>). This is a pretty huge prize, considering all you're doing is knocking something over.
* You want this prize. You want to meet the Shougun. Soon you'll both be eating shellfish together!
