# <bigfont> 招</bigfont> - Beckon 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 招 | Beckon | しょう, まね | [扌](../radicals/扌.md) (Fingers), [召](../radicals/召.md) (Call),  | [Level 27](../levels/wk_level27.md) |

Previous kanji: [占](占.md) Next kanji: [段](段.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * Someone is reaching out with their <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> fingers</span> to <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> call</span> for you. They <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> beckon</span> you closer, this way.
* Something about the way they're beckoning you with their fingers is really enticing. You can't help but walk toward them.
* The person <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> beckon</span>ing you is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Shou</span>gun (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しょう](https://jisho.org/search/しょう)</span>). You can't help but obey the Shougun's beckoning call.
* Even if the Shougun didn't have an army that would crush you if you didn't come when he called, you'd go anyway. Those beckoning fingers are just so... weirdly enticing.
