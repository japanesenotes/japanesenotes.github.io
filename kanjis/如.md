# <bigfont> 如</bigfont> - Likeness 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 如 | Likeness | じょ, ごと | [女](../radicals/女.md) (Woman), [口](../radicals/口.md) (Mouth),  | [Level 47](../levels/wk_level47.md) |

Previous kanji: [墜](墜.md) Next kanji: [婆](婆.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * With a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> woman</span>'s <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span>, you have a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> likeness</span> to me.
* Imagine someone's putting a woman's mouth over your mouth, looking at you, and commenting on your likeness. All it took was this woman's mouth.
* You have a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> likeness</span> to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Jo</span>-Anne (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [じょ](https://jisho.org/search/じょ)</span>) when you wear that mouth.
* Jo-Anne popped her mouth off and put it on you. Indeed with that mouth on, you have an extreme likeness to her.
