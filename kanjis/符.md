# <bigfont> 符</bigfont> - Token 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous kanji: [漏](漏.md) Next kanji: [購](購.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * You take some <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> bamboo</span> and <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> attach</span> it together into a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> token</span> of appreciation for your friend. Your friend greatly appreciates this token of friendship.
* Imagine trying to attach some bamboo together into a token for someone.
* You hand over the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> token</span> but your friend drops it and yells, You <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> foo</span>l (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ふ](https://jisho.org/search/ふ)</span>)! I'm allergic to bamboo!
* Imagine the embarrassment you feel, forgetting they were allergic to bamboo. Oh well, it’s the thought that counts. You’re still a fool, though.
