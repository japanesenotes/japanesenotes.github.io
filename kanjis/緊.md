## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> servant</span> sits on a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> stool</span>, holding a piece of <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> thread</span>, looking very <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> tense</span>. 
* You walk by this tense servant and pause for a moment. They raise their tense eyes up at you and you can see they're shaking.
* You ask the servant why they're so <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> tense</span>, and they say they've been assigned to assassinate the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Kin</span>g (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [きん](https://jisho.org/search/きん)</span>) of England with this piece of thread.
* Imagine how tense you would be if you were about to try to murder the King! Your entire body would tense up, maybe to the point that you wouldn't be able to actually go through with it.
