## Mnemonics
 * This is a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> gold</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> roof</span>, <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> correct</span>? It's important to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> lock</span> it up, then.
* It's only natural that you'd want to lock a gold roof so nobody can steal it.
* The person who <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> lock</span>ed the gold roof was <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Jou</span>rm (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [じょう](https://jisho.org/search/じょう)</span>).
