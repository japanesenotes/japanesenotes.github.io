## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> jammed in</span> radical and the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> jammed in</span> kanji are the same!
* Know your radicals, yo!
* Look down! You're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> jammed in</span>to something. You can't move your feet. What is this?! Oh, your body's been jammed in a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ka</span>yak (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かい](https://jisho.org/search/かい)</span>).
* Picture yourself jammed into a kayak. Your pants are bunching and sticking out the sides and top. You really don't fit in here.
