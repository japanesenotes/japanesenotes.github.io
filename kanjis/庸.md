## Mnemonics
 * Under this <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> canopy</span> you have a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> brush</span> and a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> task</span> to complete with it. This is totally <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> common</span>. 
* This type of thing is common for almost every class or job you'll ever have. They sit you down under the canopy, put a brush in your hand, and say, Here complete this task. I'll be over there.
* This is so <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> common</span> and familiar to you that you finish your task early and eat some <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> yo</span>gurt (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [よう](https://jisho.org/search/よう)</span>) while you wait for the guy to come back.
* As you eat the yogurt you think about how common yogurt is too. It's just a normal food, common in everyone's fridge.
