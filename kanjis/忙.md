# <bigfont> 忙</bigfont> - Busy 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 忙 | Busy | ぼう, いそが | [忄](../radicals/忄.md) (Soul), [亡](../radicals/亡.md) (Death),  | [Level 19](../levels/wk_level19.md) |

Previous kanji: [宙](宙.md) Next kanji: [履](履.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * You'll work your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> soul</span> to <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> death</span> if you're too <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> busy</span>. Being busy just isn't good for your soul. Take breaks. Go on vacation. Don't be so busy that you destroy yourself.
* Look around you. See all those busy people? Their souls are dying. Don't end up like them.
* Take time out of your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> busy</span> life and go <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bow</span>ling (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぼう](https://jisho.org/search/ぼう)</span>) once in a while. In fact, you should probably go bowling once a week at least. Nothing brings energy back to your life like some relaxing bowling.
* Think about your busy schedule this week. Now cancel everything and just go bowling instead.
