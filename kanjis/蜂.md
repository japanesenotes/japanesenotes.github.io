# <bigfont> 蜂</bigfont> - Bee

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous kanji: [蛍](蛍.md) Next kanji: [酎](酎.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * On the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> insect</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> signpost</span> there is a single sign that says <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bee</span>.
* This particular insect signpost points you to where the bee is.
* Thanks to the insect signpost you find the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bee</span>. You make it just in time to see the bee <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hatch</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はち](https://jisho.org/search/はち)</span>) from its egg.
* You got to see a single bee hatch! What a beautiful sight. Weird that a signpost points to it.
