# <bigfont> 組</bigfont> - Group, Association, Team 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 組 | Group, Association, Team | そ, くみ | [糸](../radicals/糸.md) (Thread), [且](../radicals/且.md) (Top Hat),  | [Level 7](../levels/wk_level7.md) |

Previous kanji: [理](理.md) Next kanji: [船](船.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * Sew <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> thread</span> through your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> top hat</span> and to the top hats of other people you know and you'll turn into a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> group</span> of top hat wearing people. You're all connected by the thread now, so everyone will assume you're a group, even if that's all you guys have going for yourselves.
* Picture a group of people threaded together by their top hats. Don't you want to be part of a cool group like that? Your own top hat team.
* This <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> group</span> decides to make <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> so</span>ap (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [そ](https://jisho.org/search/そ)</span>) together. You take off your top hats and use them as soap molds, mixing together all of your favorite smells and colors.
* Imagine being in a group of soap makers. Picture the soap bubbles floating between all of you.
