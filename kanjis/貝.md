## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> shellfish</span> radical and the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shellfish</span> kanji are the same! It can be just the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shell</span> part too.
* Shellfish are shellfish, you know?
* To get some <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shellfish</span>, all you have to do is hop in your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ka</span>yak (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かい](https://jisho.org/search/かい)</span>) and get some. Shellfish live in the water.
* Imagine rowing your kayak out into the ocean to collect shellfish. Imagine the color and shape of the kayak. Feel the spray of the salt water and the taste of some fresh shellfish, right out of the ocean.
