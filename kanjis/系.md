## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> lineage</span> radical and the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> lineage</span> kanji are the same.
* Know your radicals, yo!
* You trace your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> lineage</span> back and realize that every single person in your family loved <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ca</span>ke (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [けい](https://jisho.org/search/けい)</span>). Most of them were cake bakers, too!
* You come from a lineage of cake makers and eaters. No wonder you love cake so much! (And if you hate cake, no wonder your family doesn't like you.)
