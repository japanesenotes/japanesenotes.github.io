# <bigfont> 契</bigfont> - Pledge 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 契 | Pledge | けい | [生](../radicals/生.md) (Life), [刀](../radicals/刀.md) (Sword), [大](../radicals/大.md) (Big),  | [Level 37](../levels/wk_level37.md) |

Previous kanji: [削](削.md) Next kanji: [恵](恵.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * The weight of <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> life</span> on your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> sword</span> is <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> big</span> when you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> pledge</span> to protect your city. That's the big, big weight of every single person's life in that city pressing down on you every time you take out that sword.
* Imagine holding up your sword and pledging to protect the lives of everyone in this city. It's a big deal!
* Until the city is safe, you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> pledge</span> to give up <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ca</span>ke (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [けい](https://jisho.org/search/けい)</span>), your favorite food.
* Would you pledge to give up cake to show everyone how serious you are about protecting life?
