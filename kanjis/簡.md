| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics
 * Keeping at a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> bamboo</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> interval</span> is the definition of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> simplicity</span>. 
* Just one bamboo wide, over and over. That is simplicity.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Simplicity</span> is what Genghis <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Khan</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かん](https://jisho.org/search/かん)</span>) always demanded. Everything he decided was based on simplicity.
* Simplicity is how he took over so much of the world. If he didn't stick to simplicity, there's no way he'd have been able to do what he did.
