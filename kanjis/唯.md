## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span> of that <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> turkey</span> is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> solely</span> his. It’s very unique, so that mouth could belong to no other turkey but him. That mouth is solely his.
* Imagine what makes this turkey’s mouth so unique. This beak is solely his. It belongs to no other. This turkey is the only turkey with a mouth like his.
* You traveled a long way to see this turkey and the mouth that is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> solely</span> his but you missed your exit on the high way and you have to hang a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> U-ey</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ゆい](https://jisho.org/search/ゆい)</span>). You know what hang a U-ey means, right? It’s making a U-turn. Well, you have to hang a U-ey on the highway so you can get off at the right spot to see this turkey and the mouth that is solely his.
* Imagine hanging a U-ey on the highway. Hear your tires screech as you turn around and get off where you’re supposed to.
