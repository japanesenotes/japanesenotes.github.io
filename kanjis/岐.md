# <bigfont> 岐</bigfont> - Branch Off 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 岐 | Branch Off | き, ぎ | [山](../radicals/山.md) (Mountain), [支](../radicals/支.md) (Branch),  | [Level 39](../levels/wk_level39.md) |

Previous kanji: [阜](阜.md) Next kanji: [隆](隆.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mountain</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> branch</span> will <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> branch off</span> from the rest.
* Picture this part of the mountain, branching off from the rest, into a mysterious path of its own.
* If you want to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> branch off</span> from your hiking group and explore this branch of the mountain, you need a special <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> key</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [き](https://jisho.org/search/き)</span>). Just don't get caught down there or you'll get the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> gui</span>llotine (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぎ](https://jisho.org/search/ぎ)</span>).
* If you branch off from the beaten path, you need 1) access, and 2) to be really careful so you keep your head.
