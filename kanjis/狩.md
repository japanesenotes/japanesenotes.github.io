## Mnemonics
 * I <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> protect</span> my own <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> animal</span>s by going to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hunt</span> other animals... like wolves and cougars and such. 
* My animals can't protect themselves, that's why I hunt other animals instead.
* I also do all my <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hunt</span>s using a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ca</span>r (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [か](https://jisho.org/search/か)</span>). I just run those animals down with my car. Kind of cheating, but vroom vroom anyways.
* Imagine hunting down animals in a car.
