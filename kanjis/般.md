## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> boat</span> will have at least one <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> weapon</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> generally</span>.
* In general, you can assume a boat will have a weapon, but not all of them will. 
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Generally</span> a boat with a weapon will be owned by <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Han</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はん](https://jisho.org/search/はん)</span>) Solo. 
* So of all the boats out there, generally all of them have weapons, and generally Han Solo will be on one.
