| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 遊 | Play | ゆう, あそ | [方](../radicals/方.md) (Direction), [子](../radicals/子.md) (Child),  | [Level 30](../levels/wk_level30.md) |

Previous kanji: [婚](婚.md) Next kanji: [旗](旗.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * On a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> scooter</span> running in one <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> direction</span> is a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> gun</span>-toting <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> child</span>. This is a terribly dangerous situation, but to the child it is just <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> play</span>.
* Imagine a child riding a scooter with a gun having a good time. Come play with me! they shout. No! you yell back.
* Everything is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> play</span> for <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> you</span>ths (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ゆう](https://jisho.org/search/ゆう)</span>), even if it's a terrible, scary, not good situation like this one.
* Imagine trying to explain to the youth that this is not play and that they should stop, but them youths can't stop playing.<br />P.S. From the WaniKani staff to you: Don't let youths play with guns, please.
