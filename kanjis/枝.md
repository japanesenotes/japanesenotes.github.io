## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> tree</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> branch</span> is exactly what it sounds like: the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> branch</span> of a tree.
* Picture a tree branch in your hands. Feel it. Smell it. It smells and feels like a tree.
* Take this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> branch</span> and give it to a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shee</span>p (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [し](https://jisho.org/search/し)</span>). Sheep are like dogs, they like to play fetch with tree branches. And now you're playing fetch with this sheep and this branch.
* Imagine playing fetch with a tree branch and a sheep. Man's second best friend.
