## Mnemonics
 * This <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> bamboo</span> can be whittled into an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> example</span> of anything. See, here's a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> car</span>. Here's a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> snake</span>. I can make so many examples just with bamboo.
* It's amazing how fast this guy can whittle!
* One last <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> example</span> for you. Now I'm going to take this bamboo and whittle it into an example of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Han</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はん](https://jisho.org/search/はん)</span>) Solo!
* Han Solo's bamboo example comes to life and runs off.
