# <bigfont> 痩</bigfont> - Thin 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 痩 | Thin | そう, や | [疒](../radicals/疒.md) (Sick), [申](../radicals/申.md) (Humble), [又](../radicals/又.md) (Stool),  | [Level 34](../levels/wk_level34.md) |

Previous kanji: [濡](濡.md) Next kanji: [羨](羨.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * You are so <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> sick</span> that you've come to look like the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> humble</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> stool</span>: <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> thin</span>. Eerily thin, in fact. 
* Imagine standing behind a stool and being able to disappear behind it completely. That's how thin this sickness has made you.
* When you're this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> thin</span>, your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sou</span>l (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [そう](https://jisho.org/search/そう)</span>) is visible from outside of your body too.
* Picture your soul pulsing through your thin body. You have no body fat to cover it up, so everyone can see it pulsing and lurking in there. This is why you're hiding behind one of the humble stools in your house.
