## Mnemonics
 * Your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> shellfish</span> are worth a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> fortune</span> because you decided to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> paste</span> your fortune to the shellfish.
* Picture all of your riches, your money, your jewels. They're all pasted directly onto all of your shellfish!
* You're a little crazy, which is why you decided to use <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> paste</span> to paste your entire fortune to these shellfish, and as you paste each one you yell out, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Ha</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [は](https://jisho.org/search/は)</span>)! Paste.
* Ha ha! Paste paste. Ha ha ha!! PASTE PASTE PASTE.
