## Mnemonics
 * <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> Imperial</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> clothes</span> allow you to perform an ancient <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> attack</span> that cannot be repelled.
* IMPERIAL ATTACK, GO! Imagine what this imperial attack might look like and imagine yourself in some swanky imperial clothes while you perform it.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> attack</span>'s power is concentrated in the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shoe</span>s (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しゅう](https://jisho.org/search/しゅう)</span>) in this imperial outfit. You attack with your feet, kicking the enemy with your old, fancy shoes.
* Picture the shoes as you attack, they glow—full of attack power! These +4 attack shoes are really special.
