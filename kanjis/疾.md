## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> sick</span>ness that hits you like an <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> arrow</span> will do its gross work <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> rapidly</span>. It's not a good thing, you're gonna get really sick really fast.
* Picture this rapid sickness spreading through your body like a cartoon arrow ricocheting through your body. Yuck!
* This <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> rapid</span> sickness makes your head pound like there's a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shih tzu</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しつ](https://jisho.org/search/しつ)</span>) yapping and barking nonstop. And it's making you sh*t a lot.
* Imagine getting sick this rapidly. All of a sudden, there's a shih tzu barking in your head, it hurts! And... oh no, where's the toilet?!
