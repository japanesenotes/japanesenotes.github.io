## Mnemonics
 * Who will <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> say</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> turkey</span>? Who? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> WHO</span>???
* Say who over and over again, demanding someone says turkey, for some reason.
* Then, someone says turkey. <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Who</span> said turkey? You? How <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dare</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [だれ](https://jisho.org/search/だれ)</span>) you.
* You're so mad at that person even though you demanded someone say turkey.
