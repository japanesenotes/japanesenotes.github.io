# <bigfont> 懸</bigfont> - Suspend 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 懸 | Suspend | けん, か | [県](../radicals/県.md) (Prefecture), [系](../radicals/系.md) (Lineage), [心](../radicals/心.md) (Heart),  | [Level 38](../levels/wk_level38.md) |

Previous kanji: [慮](慮.md) Next kanji: [房](房.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * In your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> prefecture</span>, you come from a long <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> lineage</span> of people who share one <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> heart</span>. When the heart is passed on to the next in line, it flies out of their body, and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> suspend</span>s itself in the sky, for you to take.
* You see the suspended heart in the distance, and make your way towards it.
* You arrive at the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> suspend</span>ed heart. There in front of it, protecting it, is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Ken</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [けん](https://jisho.org/search/けん)</span>) the samurai. 
* I've protected this suspended heart for you, for you are next in the lineage to receive it. He grabs the heart and throws it into your body.
