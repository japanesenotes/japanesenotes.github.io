## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> leader</span> with the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> old</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span> is the one who has said many things. That makes them different from everyone, an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> individual</span>.
* Imagine that leader's old mouth saying things that make the old man stand apart from the others. They are individual from everyone else.
* As an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> individual</span>, one must teach all the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> 子</span>s (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こ](https://jisho.org/search/こ)</span>) their way. Their individual way.
* You must be your own individual. Don't be a sheeple, he says to the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [子](https://jisho.org/search/子)</span>s.
