# <bigfont> 防</bigfont> - Prevent, Prevention 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 防 | Prevent, Prevention | ぼう, ふせ | [阝](../radicals/阝.md) (Building), [方](../radicals/方.md) (Direction),  | [Level 20](../levels/wk_level20.md) |

Previous kanji: [罪](罪.md) Next kanji: [穴](穴.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * If there is a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> building</span> in the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> direction</span> you want to go it will <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> prevent</span> you from going any further. Like, come on kid, you think you can just walk right through a building? Don't make me laugh!
* There's a building right there. Try to go that direction. Oh, did the building prevent you? Told you so.
* The building is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> prevent</span>ing you from going the right direction, so you pull a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bow</span>ling (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぼう](https://jisho.org/search/ぼう)</span>) ball out of your bag, aim, and bowl the building over. The building, and all the ones around it, go tumbling down. But now the rubble is preventing you from going further.
* Your bowling antics are now preventing a bunch of people from going the directions they want to go. Nice job.
