## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> big</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> turkey</span> in the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> rice paddy</span> is getting all <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> stirred up</span>. He's kicking up all the rice. Who stirred him up?
* He is a really big turkey too. Like, 12 feet tall, or something. Don't want to stir up a turkey that's that big.
* While the turkey is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> stirred up</span>, it also looks like it's having a lot of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> fun</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ふん](https://jisho.org/search/ふん)</span>). 
* Is it laughing? Oh no, this is bad, it's enjoying being stirred up, now we'll never stop it.
