# <bigfont> 志</bigfont> - Intention 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 志 | Intention | し, こころざし | [士](../radicals/士.md) (Samurai), [心](../radicals/心.md) (Heart),  | [Level 32](../levels/wk_level32.md) |

Previous kanji: [迎](迎.md) Next kanji: [恩](恩.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * If you can understand a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> samurai</span>'s <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> heart</span>, you'll discover their true <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> intention</span>s.
* If you're facing down a samurai, it's pretty important for you to be able to understand their intentions. Not all samurai are good, you know.
* You try to read the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> intention</span>s of the samurai in front of you and they're dark, evil, scary. Oh no! These are the intentions of the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shee</span>p (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [し](https://jisho.org/search/し)</span>) samurai!
* The sheep samurai only has violent intentions for other samurai and sheep. Its heart is dark and surprisingly easy to read.
