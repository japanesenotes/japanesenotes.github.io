## Mnemonics
 * You're in a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> net</span> but you have the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> ability</span> to quit playing along and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> leave</span> at any time. 
* Your captors think they have you trapped, but you know you can leave whenever you feel like it.
* You don't know if you want to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> quit</span> this charade until you feel an uncomfortable <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hea</span>t (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ひ](https://jisho.org/search/ひ)</span>). You look down and the net you're in is being hung above a large fire.
* As the heat spreads you decide to leave. You hop out of the net and watch the shock spread in your captors eyes.
