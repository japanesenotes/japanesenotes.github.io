Previous kanji: [兵](兵.md) Next kanji: [塩](塩.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> Viking</span> will empty his <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span> of all its <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> dirt</span> in the great <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hall</span>. They all get together in some kind of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> public chamber</span> and dish all the dirt (literal and figurative) that they've got.
* Picture a long Viking hall, with a big table and tons of chairs. All of the Vikings are gossiping and sharing all their dirt on their enemies.
* While the Vikings gather to spill the dirt in their <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hall</span>, they also stuff their faces with <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dou</span>ghnuts (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [どう](https://jisho.org/search/どう)</span>). Ah yes, Vikings love to eat doughnuts when they talk trash.
* Now picture all of the Vikings in the hall spitting dirt with one breathe and shoveling doughnuts in with the next. It's pretty gross!
