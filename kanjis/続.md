| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 続 | Continue | ぞく, つづ | [糸](../radicals/糸.md) (Thread), [売](../radicals/売.md) (Sell),  | [Level 19](../levels/wk_level19.md) |

Previous kanji: [改](改.md) Next kanji: [連](連.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * You have to <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> sell</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> thread</span> if you want to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> continue</span> to live. You want to make a living don’t you? Make sure you continue to sell that thread every single day!
* Imagine selling thread so you can continue living your life.
* You have to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> continue</span> to sell that thread because a Boso<span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> zoku</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぞく](https://jisho.org/search/ぞく)</span>) bike gang is forcing you to do it. You see? If you want to continue to live, you have to appease this Bosozoku gang. You better keep selling!
* Continue selling thread and paying off the Bosozoku. It’s the only way you can continue to survive!
