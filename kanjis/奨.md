# <bigfont> 奨</bigfont> - Encourage 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 奨 | Encourage | しょう | [将](../radicals/将.md) (Commander), [大](../radicals/大.md) (Big),  | [Level 49](../levels/wk_level49.md) |

Previous kanji: [衰](衰.md) Next kanji: [劣](劣.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> commander</span> who's <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> big</span> will use his size to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> encourage</span> his troops.
* Look at me! You can be a big commander someday too! he shouts, encouraging everyone.
* He <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> encourage</span>s the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Shou</span>gun (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しょう](https://jisho.org/search/しょう)</span>) too! You've never seen the Shougun express any emotion other than intense indifference or intense dislike.
* Picture the encouraged Shougun: his eyes are shining with tears, his heart is pounding, he's been encouraged to be better!
