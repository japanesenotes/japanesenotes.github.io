## Mnemonics
 * <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> Fire</span>, <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> fire</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> flame</span>! There’s flames everywhere!
* Feel the fire all around you, the flames licking at your body. It’s really hot!
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> flame</span>s gets bigger, surround you, and then everything <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> en</span>ds (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [えん](https://jisho.org/search/えん)</span>).
* Sorry, but standing in a blaze isn't the best idea. Flames are pretty efficient at ending the lives of the things in it. Maybe you should do this kanji lesson over and try not to stand in the flames next time.
