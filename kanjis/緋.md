## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> thread</span>s a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> criminal</span> wears will always be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> scarlet</span>. Scarlet is the color of freshly spilt blood, which is why bad guys wear it so much.
* Picture a person wearing all scarlet all the time. Don't they give you a criminal-y vibe?
* This <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> scarlet</span> color holds a lot of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> he</span>at (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ひ](https://jisho.org/search/ひ)</span>). Scarlet is the color of fire, and it has the heat of fire too.
* Picture a criminal, covered in this scarlet thread, heat radiating off of him.
