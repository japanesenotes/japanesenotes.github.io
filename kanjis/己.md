## Mnemonics
 * The kanji and the radical are the same, so remembering the meaning of this kanji is as simple as making sure you know the radical for <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> oneself</span> first! If you know the radical, you know this kanji means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> oneself</span> as well.
* Make sure you know those radicals, yo!
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Oneself</span>. Who only thinks of oneself? Every <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> 子</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こ](https://jisho.org/search/こ)</span>) in the world.
* Kids can only think of oneself. Until a certain age, that's literally all they can care about.
