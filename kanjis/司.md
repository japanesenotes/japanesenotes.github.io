## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> creeper</span> inside of a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> cape</span>, who are they? They’re the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> director</span> of a movie. They try to look all mysterious, but you know it’s just so people go see their movies and think they’re cool. Really they’re just a creep deep down.
* Picture the creepiest director you know. Wearing a cloak and cackling as he directs your favorite actors.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> director</span> is currently directing a movie about <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shee</span>p (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [し](https://jisho.org/search/し)</span>). He keeps petting them and talking to them like they're humans.
