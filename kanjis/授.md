## Mnemonics
 * There are <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> fingers</span> reaching towards you asking you to <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> accept</span> to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> instruct</span> them. They're the fingers of students. And you're the teacher. See them reach out for knowledge. They want to accept this knowledge into their hearts and brains. Instruct them!
* Picture yourself instructing a class full of knowledge hungry little kids. They reach out with their fingers to accept the truth you bring to them.
* Yes! I will <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> instruct</span> you, my dear students! you cry, I will instruct you on how to open a small <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ju</span>ice (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [じゅ](https://jisho.org/search/じゅ)</span>) box! That's right. These little kids need help learning how to open small juice boxes (small because kids are small).
* Picture yourself instructing little kids how to punch the straw into small juice boxes. Hold it right-side-up! Don't squeeze too hard! Don't punch it through the other side! No! Don't bite the box! Instructing is hard!
