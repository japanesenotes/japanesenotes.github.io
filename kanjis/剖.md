# <bigfont> 剖</bigfont> - Divide 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 剖 | Divide | ぼう | [咅](../radicals/咅.md) (Clown), [刂](../radicals/刂.md) (Knife),  | [Level 58](../levels/wk_level58.md) |

Previous kanji: [陪](陪.md) Next kanji: [譜](譜.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> clown</span> takes a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> knife</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> divide</span>s himself. First he divides himself into two pieces, then he's in four pieces, then... oh why won't he stop?!
* Instead of dying, the clown divides into slightly thinner clowns.
* The clown finally stops trying to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> divide</span> himself, and you can't stand to look at all of him. You grab a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bow</span>ling (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぼう](https://jisho.org/search/ぼう)</span>) ball and bowl all of his divided parts over!
* The many clowns crash to the floor as the bowling ball plows through their... his(?) divided bodies. You can't bear to look anymore.
