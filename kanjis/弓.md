## Mnemonics
 * The <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> bow</span> radical and the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bow</span> kanji are exactly the same!
* Make sure you know those radicals!
* You have a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bow</span> in one hand and in the other... a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cu</span>cumber (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [きゅう](https://jisho.org/search/きゅう)</span>). You're going to shoot this cucumber with this bow into the salad you're making. Many people don't know this, but this makes your cucumbers taste better.
* Imagine fitting a cucumber into the string of your bow. Imagine the smell of it as you draw the string back. Mm, take a quick bite, then let it loose.
