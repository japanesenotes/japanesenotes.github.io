# <bigfont> 諮</bigfont> - Consult 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 諮 | Consult | し, はか | [言](../radicals/言.md) (Say), [次](../radicals/次.md) (Next), [口](../radicals/口.md) (Mouth),  | [Level 50](../levels/wk_level50.md) |

Previous kanji: [訂](訂.md) Next kanji: [銘](銘.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * Watch what you <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> say</span> to the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> next</span> <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> mouth</span> over, because they hired you to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> consult</span> them.
* The person with the next mouth in the room is waiting for you to open yours to consult them. He'll do whatever you say.
* You panic, and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> consult</span> the man into buying a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shee</span>p (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [し](https://jisho.org/search/し)</span>).
* He was asking for love advice, so I guess you consulted that he give a sheep to the person he likes? Uh oh. Remind me never to consult with you when I'm feeling unsure about things.
