# <bigfont> 悲</bigfont> - Sad 

| Kanji | Meanings | Reading | Radicals | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 悲 | Sad | ひ, かな | [非](../radicals/非.md) (Criminal), [心](../radicals/心.md) (Heart),  | [Level 17](../levels/wk_level17.md) |

Previous kanji: [寒](寒.md) Next kanji: [暑](暑.md) 

## Mnemonics
 * A <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> criminal</span>'s <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> heart</span> will always be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sad</span>. No one chooses a life of crime if they're happy. And even if they seem like they're doing fine on the outside, deep down they're really sad.
* Imagine watching a criminal after he's done stealing from a bank. He's all alone. He stops throwing the money around. And cries. So so sad.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sad</span> feelings fill the criminal's body with <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> he</span>at (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ひ](https://jisho.org/search/ひ)</span>). This heat spreads and radiates out. If you walk next to them on the street, you'll feel the sad heat floating by you.
* Imagine being so sad that your entire body fills with heat. That heat even makes squiggly heat lines pop out above and around you.
