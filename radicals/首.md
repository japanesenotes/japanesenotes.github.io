# <bigfont> 首</bigfont> - Neck 

Previous kanji: [会](会.md) Next kanji: [ユ](ユ.md) 

## Meanings
 Neck
## Mnemonics
 * There are some horns on the ground that you want for yourself. You pick them up and you attach them to your <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> neck</span>. Imagine these horns sticking out of your neck. No one can slit your throat now that your neck is protected.
