# <bigfont> 舎</bigfont> - Cottage 

Previous kanji: [胃](胃.md) Next kanji: [凸](凸.md) 

## Meanings
 Cottage
## Mnemonics
 * This radical looks like a little house, one that would be perfect in the middle of nowhere, surrounded by bamboo and a little mountain stream. See the roof and the open door? That's why this is the <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> cottage</span> radical.
