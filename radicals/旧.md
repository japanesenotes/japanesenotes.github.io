## Mnemonics
 * There’s a&nbsp;<span class=radical-highlight rel=tooltip title=Radical>stick&nbsp;next to the&nbsp;<span class=radical-highlight rel=tooltip title=Radical>sun, reflecting on its&nbsp;<span class=kanji-highlight rel=tooltip title=Kanji>former&nbsp;glory.
