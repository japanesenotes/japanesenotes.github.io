# <bigfont> 坴</bigfont> - Womb 

Previous kanji: [意](意.md) Next kanji: [丸](丸.md) 

## Meanings
 Womb
## Mnemonics
 * Legs appear between two lumps of dirt. This is the earth's version of a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> womb</span>. Imagine a tiny baby growing under the ground and then sprouting out, legs first, into the world. Imagine being inside this dirt womb. It's warm and damp. You feel safe.
