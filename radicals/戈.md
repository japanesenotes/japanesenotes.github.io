# <bigfont> 戈</bigfont> - Drunkard 

Previous kanji: [及](及.md) Next kanji: [皮](皮.md) 

## Meanings
 Drunkard
## Mnemonics
 * This is a ceremony radical with a slide radical. Why would someone be sliding on the floor at a ceremony? If they're a <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> drunkard</span>, that's why! People love to drink at ceremonies. And there's always at least one drunkard who takes it too far and slides across the floor doing the worm.
