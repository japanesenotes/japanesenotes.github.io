## Mnemonics
 * I know you think the left part is ride, but that is not correct. It is a cow, and below the cow there is a towel, to the right there is a knife. The cow uses the towel and knife to CONTROL the world.<br><br>Cows, using nothing more than&nbsp;towels&nbsp;and&nbsp;knifes, have taken&nbsp;control&nbsp;of the world. Their&nbsp;systemof control is brutal—payback for how us humans have treated them I guess.
