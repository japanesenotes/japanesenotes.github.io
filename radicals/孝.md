# <bigfont> 孝</bigfont> - Teacher 

Previous kanji: [台](台.md) Next kanji: [且](且.md) 

## Meanings
 Teacher
## Mnemonics
 * This radical is made up of coffin and child. To keep a child out of a coffin they'll need a good <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> teacher</span> to make sure they know not to stick forks in outlets or dance in traffic. That's why this radical is a teacher.
