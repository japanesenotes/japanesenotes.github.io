# <bigfont> 夌</bigfont> - Frostbite 

Previous kanji: [禹](禹.md) Next kanji: [夋](夋.md) 

## Meanings
 Frostbite
## Mnemonics
 * This radical is made of dirt, legs, and winter. If you have to bury your legs in the dirt in winter, it's probably because you got <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> frostbite</span>. You had to leave your dead legs behind. :(
