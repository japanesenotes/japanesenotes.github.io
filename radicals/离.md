## Mnemonics
 * This looks like ROCKET man.<br><br>This is a very complicated and tall radical. It has a lid on top to keep the explosiveness in. It also contains a treasure, at least for the people who will receive what's inside. Also, it has a cow, which will be used for milk on Mars.
