# <bigfont> 刃</bigfont> - Blade 

Previous kanji: [缶](缶.md) Next kanji: [竜](竜.md) 

## Meanings
 Blade
## Mnemonics
 * You have a sword that has a drop of something on it. That drop is a drop of blood. Sure, a sword will cut someone to spill some blood, but all you really need is any kind of <span style="background-color:#ADD8E6"> blade</span> to cut someone. Sword, knife, whatever, as long as it's a blade.
