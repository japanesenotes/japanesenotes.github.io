## Mnemonics:

* 
* The kanji is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> boisterous</span>. The verb version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to make noise</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be boisterous</span>. Basically, you're being boisterous when you make a lot of noise, so that's where it comes from.
* When you go <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to make noise</span> and be boisterous, you go to the local <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [沢](https://jisho.org/search/沢)</span> (<span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> さわ</span>) swamp. It's an awesome place, and people don't complain about the noise.
