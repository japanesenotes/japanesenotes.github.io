## Mnemonics:
ビエトはコウイチに、Tofuguが成功する確率は二百パーセントだと言いきった。
* Viet told Koichi that the chances of Tofugu succeeding were 200%.
* You're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> certain</span> there is this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> percent</span> chance it will happen? You know that this is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> probability</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 確, 率
* Certain, Percent
