| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> listen</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to listen to</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to hear</span>.
* The meaning is really similar to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [聞く](https://jisho.org/search/聞く)</span>, right? The reading is the same.
