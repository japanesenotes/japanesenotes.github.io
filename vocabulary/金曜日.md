# <bigfont> 金曜日</bigfont> - Friday 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 金曜日 | Friday | きんようび |  [金](../kanjis/金.md) (Gold), [曜](../kanjis/曜.md) (Weekday, Day Of The Week), [日](../kanjis/日.md) (Sun, Day) | [Level 18](../levels/wk_level18.md) |

Previous vocab: [火曜日](火曜日.md) Next vocab: [可能性](可能性.md) 

## Mnemonics:
「フグのコスチュームですが、いつ頃仕上がりますかねえ？」「金曜日の午後までにはできていると思います。」
* When will my Fugu costume be ready? It’ll be ready by Friday afternoon.
* This is a day of the week. You know this because it has <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [曜日](https://jisho.org/search/曜日)</span> in it. But what day of the week is it? The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> gold</span> day of the week is the greatest day. It's the day that makes you feel like gold because it's the end of the weekdays and the beginning of the weekend! This day is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Friday</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. It will help to know the word <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [曜日](https://jisho.org/search/曜日)</span> as well.
* 金, 曜, 日
* Gold, Weekday, Sun
