## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> luxuriant</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be luxuriant</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to grow thickly</span>. 
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> She ga</span>ve (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しげ](https://jisho.org/search/しげ)</span>) me a razor because my beard was starting <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to grow thickly</span>... too thickly. But, I won't shave my awesome beard no matter what she gave me.
