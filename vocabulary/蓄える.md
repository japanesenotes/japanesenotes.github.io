## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. * We need to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> talk, wha</span>t (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [たくわ](https://jisho.org/search/たくわ)</span>) are you doing with all those? Are you amassing them? Why?
