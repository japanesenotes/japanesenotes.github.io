# <bigfont> 揚げる</bigfont> - To Hoist, To Fly, To Raise, To Deep Fry 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 揚げる | To Hoist, To Fly, To Raise, To Deep Fry | あげる |  [揚](../kanjis/揚.md) (Hoist) | [Level 42](../levels/wk_level42.md) |

Previous vocab: [発掘](発掘.md) Next vocab: [握る](握る.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hoist</span>. This strange word means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to hoist</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to fly</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to raise</span>, and (most importantly) <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to deep fry</span>. The hoisting part usually has to do with hoisting or raising a flag, or something similar.
* Remember the other to raise vocabs? They are all <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [あげる](https://jisho.org/search/あげる)</span> as well. For example, <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [上げる](https://jisho.org/search/上げる)</span> is <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [あげる](https://jisho.org/search/あげる)</span> and <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [挙げる](https://jisho.org/search/挙げる)</span> is <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [あげる](https://jisho.org/search/あげる)</span> as well.
