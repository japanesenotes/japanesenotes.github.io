# <bigfont> 慣れる</bigfont> - To Become Accustomed To, To Get Used To 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 慣れる | To Become Accustomed To, To Get Used To | なれる |  [慣](../kanjis/慣.md) (Accustomed) | [Level 25](../levels/wk_level25.md) |

Previous vocab: [金属](金属.md) Next vocab: [習慣](習慣.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> accustomed</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to become accustomed</span>.
* What have you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> become accustomed to</span>? All the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Na</span>rwhals (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [な](https://jisho.org/search/な)</span>) you hang out with. They take some getting used to.
