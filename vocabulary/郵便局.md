## Mnemonics:

* 
* With <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [郵便](https://jisho.org/search/郵便)</span> being <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mail</span> and <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [局](https://jisho.org/search/局)</span> being bureau, we have the mail bureau. They're more often known as the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> post office</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. Will help to know the word <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [郵便](https://jisho.org/search/郵便)</span> as well.
* 郵, 便, 局
* Mail, Convenience, Bureau
