## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> weave</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to weave</span>.
* When you have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to weave</span> the only thing you can weave is an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> O</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [お](https://jisho.org/search/お)</span>) shape. That's it. Just O's.
