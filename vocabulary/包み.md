## Mnemonics:

* 
* Something that you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> wrap</span> is a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bundle</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> package</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: What's better than one <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bundle</span>? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Two</span> bundles. <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Two</span> packages. (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つつ](https://jisho.org/search/つつ)</span>). Two times two is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> つつ</span>.
