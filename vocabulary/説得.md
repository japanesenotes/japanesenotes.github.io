# <bigfont> 説得</bigfont> - Persuasion, Convince 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 説得 | Persuasion, Convince | せっとく |  [説](../kanjis/説.md) (Theory), [得](../kanjis/得.md) (Acquire) | [Level 19](../levels/wk_level19.md) |

Previous vocab: [得意](得意.md) Next vocab: [忙しい](忙しい.md) 

## Mnemonics:
日本に住むために、まず最初に自分の親を説得しなくてはいけません。
* In order to live in Japan, the first thing I have to do is persuade my parents to let me do it.
* You <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> acquire</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> theory</span> after theory. In doing this, you have a lot of theories in your head. You use all these theories as tools for <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> persuasion</span>. These theories will allow you to convince anyone of anything.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [説](https://jisho.org/search/説)</span> gets shortened to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [せっ](https://jisho.org/search/せっ)</span>.
* 説, 得
