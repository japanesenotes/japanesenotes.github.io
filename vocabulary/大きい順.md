# <bigfont> 大きい順</bigfont> - Decreasing Order, Descending Order, Largest First 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 大きい順 | Decreasing Order, Descending Order, Largest First | おおきいじゅん |  [大](../kanjis/大.md) (Big, Large), [順](../kanjis/順.md) (Order, Sequence) | [Level 19](../levels/wk_level19.md) |

Previous vocab: [順番](順番.md) Next vocab: [小さい順](小さい順.md) 

## Mnemonics:
モデル達の写真を顔が大きい順に並べました。
* We lined up the pictures of the models faces, largest first.
* When the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> order</span> is from <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> big</span> (to small), you are talking about <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> decreasing order</span>. That's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> largest first</span>.
* The reading is the word <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [大きい](https://jisho.org/search/大きい)</span> plus <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [順](https://jisho.org/search/順)</span>'s reading you learned with the kanji.
* 大, 順
* Big, Order
