## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> praise</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to praise</span>.
* If you are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to praise</span> this guy in front of you, it would have to be for his amazing <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hoe</span>-using (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ほ](https://jisho.org/search/ほ)</span>) abilities. Look at him go with that hoe.
* 褒
