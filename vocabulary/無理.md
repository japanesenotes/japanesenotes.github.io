# <bigfont> 無理</bigfont> - Impossible, Unreasonable, Overdoing 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 無理 | Impossible, Unreasonable, Overdoing | むり |  [無](../kanjis/無.md) (Nothing), [理](../kanjis/理.md) (Reason) | [Level 17](../levels/wk_level17.md) |

Previous vocab: [兵](兵.md) Next vocab: [側](側.md) 

## Mnemonics:
自分の肘を舐めるのは無理です。
* It’s impossible to lick your own elbow.
* If you can do <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nothing</span> within <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> reason</span>, then what you're trying to do is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> unreasonable</span> and probably downright <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> impossible</span>. If you still try, you're probably just <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> overdoing</span> it.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. As long as you know the readings for these kanji, you should be able to read this word just fine.
* 無, 理
* Nothing, Reason
