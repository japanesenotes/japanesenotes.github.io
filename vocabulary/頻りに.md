## Mnemonics:

* 
* Something that goes towards being <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> frequent</span> is something that is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> frequently</span> happening.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: Something that happens <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> frequently</span> is something that has really caught on. People want to do this thing frequently, because it is so <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> chic</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しき](https://jisho.org/search/しき)</span>).
