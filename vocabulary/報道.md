| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [報じる](報じる.md) Next vocab: [お守り](お守り.md) 

## Mnemonics:
報道によると、本日早朝に起きた玉突き事故で、20匹の魚が地域の病院へ運ばれたそうだよ。
* According to the news report, twenty fish were transported to a local hospital after a pileup that occurred early this morning.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> way</span> of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> news</span> is to do lots of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> news report</span>s.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 報, 道
* News, Road
