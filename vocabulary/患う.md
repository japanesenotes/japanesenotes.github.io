## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> afflicted</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be afflicted</span>.
* I was afflicted with an ancient Egyptian disease that turned me into sun god Ra, but now I'm okay. I <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> was a Ra</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [わずら](https://jisho.org/search/わずら)</span>), but now I'm just me again.
