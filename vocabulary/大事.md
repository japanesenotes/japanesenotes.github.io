# <bigfont> 大事</bigfont> - Important, Valuable, Serious Matter 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 大事 | Important, Valuable, Serious Matter | だいじ |  [大](../kanjis/大.md) (Big, Large), [事](../kanjis/事.md) (Action, Matter, Thing) | [Level 9](../levels/wk_level9.md) |

Previous vocab: [工事](工事.md) Next vocab: [返事](返事.md) 

## Mnemonics:
リラックスする時間をとることは、とても大事だよ。
* Taking time to relax is important.
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> big</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> action</span> means something big is going down. This is no ordinary action. It is an action that is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> important</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 大, 事
* Big, Action
