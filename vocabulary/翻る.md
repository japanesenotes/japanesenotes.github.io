## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> flip</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to flip</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to turn over</span>.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> here guy</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ひるがえ](https://jisho.org/search/ひるがえ)</span>) is the guy who's here right now. He's here, and he's ready <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to flip</span> over.
