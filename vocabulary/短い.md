## Mnemonics:
わぁ！あんたの尻尾が私より短いなんて、知らなかったわ。
* Wah! I didn’t know your tail was shorter than mine.
* This is a single kanji with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [い](https://jisho.org/search/い)</span> on the end, meaning you know it's probably an adjective. What's the adjective form of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> short</span>? It's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> short</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: Please accept my apology in advance for this one, but if you're very <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> short</span> you may be a midget. And if you're a midget you probably drive a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> midge</span>t <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ca</span>r (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [みじか](https://jisho.org/search/みじか)</span>).
