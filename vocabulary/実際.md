## Mnemonics:

* On this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb">  occasion</span> it is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> truth</span>. It <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> actually</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> really</span> is the truth.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
