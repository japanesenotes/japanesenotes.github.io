## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> tighten</span>, so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to tighten</span>.
* Right now, you're trying <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to tighten</span> the wool on this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shee</span>p (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [し](https://jisho.org/search/し)</span>).
