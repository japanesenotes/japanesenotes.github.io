## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> do</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to do</span>. This is a polite way to say to do.
* What are you going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to do</span>? You're going to become a professional <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> eater</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いた](https://jisho.org/search/いた)</span>), because you like to do eating a lot, and you are very polite about your eating despite how fast you eat in these competitions.
