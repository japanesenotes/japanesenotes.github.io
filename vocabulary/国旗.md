## Mnemonics:

* 
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> country</span>'s <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> flag</span> is the flag they use for their nation. It's the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> national flag</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こく](https://jisho.org/search/こく)</span> gets shortened to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こっ](https://jisho.org/search/こっ)</span>, though, so look out for that.
* 国, 旗
* Country, Flag
