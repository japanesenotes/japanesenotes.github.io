## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> worship</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to worship</span>.
* You have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to worship</span> the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ogre</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [おが](https://jisho.org/search/おが)</span>), because if you don't it'll eat your face off.
