| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> burn</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to burn something</span> (or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to burn</span>).
* This is when you're burning something. To remember this meaning, you have to remember the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [も](https://jisho.org/search/も)</span> part. So, just think of your friend <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Mo</span>e (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [も](https://jisho.org/search/も)</span>), and how you're going to burn him... uh... just a little bit.
* 燃
* Burn
