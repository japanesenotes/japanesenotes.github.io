# <bigfont> 天国</bigfont> - Kingdom Of Heaven, Heaven, Paradise 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 天国 | Kingdom Of Heaven, Heaven, Paradise | てんごく |  [天](../kanjis/天.md) (Heaven), [国](../kanjis/国.md) (Country) | [Level 6](../levels/wk_level6.md) |

Previous vocab: [中国](中国.md) Next vocab: [全国](全国.md) 

## Mnemonics:
タバコを天国で吸いましょう。
* Let’s smoke cigarettes in Heaven.
* This is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> heaven</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> country</span>. Where is that? Probably just in <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> heaven</span> (or if you want to be fancy, the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> kingdom of heaven</span>).
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [国](https://jisho.org/search/国)</span> is changed to the reading <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ごく](https://jisho.org/search/ごく)</span>, though because of rendaku. Even heaven changes a little bit.
* 天, 国
* Heaven, Country
