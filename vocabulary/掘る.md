## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dig</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to dig</span>.
* If you are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to dig</span> you are going to end up making a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ho</span>le (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ほ](https://jisho.org/search/ほ)</span>).
