| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> limit</span> is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nothing</span>. What? No limit? That means it will never stop! It is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> infinite</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
