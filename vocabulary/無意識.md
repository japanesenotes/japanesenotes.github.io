# <bigfont> 無意識</bigfont> - Unconsciousness, Unconsciously, Involuntary 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 無意識 | Unconsciousness, Unconsciously, Involuntary | むいしき |  [無](../kanjis/無.md) (Nothing), [意](../kanjis/意.md) (Idea), [識](../kanjis/識.md) (Discerning, Discriminating, Know) | [Level 19](../levels/wk_level19.md) |

Previous vocab: [自動詞](自動詞.md) Next vocab: [私財](私財.md) 

## Mnemonics:
我々は素晴らしい。何故なら、無意識にクリエイティブになることができるからだ。
* We’re awesome because we can be unconsciously creative.
* Know how <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [意識](https://jisho.org/search/意識)</span> is consciousness? What about when this <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> consciousness</span> doesn't exist (aka it is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nothing</span>)? That's when it becomes <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> unconsciousness</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. It will help to know how to read <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [意識](https://jisho.org/search/意識)</span>, though.
* 無, 意, 識
* Nothing, Idea, Discerning
