## Mnemonics:

* 
* I'm going to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cut off</span> the part of me that I'm trying to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> diagnose</span>... well, not literally cut off, but cut off my diagnosing. I have come to a conclusion. A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> diagnosis</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
