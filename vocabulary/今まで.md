# <bigfont> 今まで</bigfont> - Until Now, Up To Now, So Far 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 今まで | Until Now, Up To Now, So Far | いままで |  [今](../kanjis/今.md) (Now) | [Level 5](../levels/wk_level5.md) |

Previous vocab: [年](年.md) Next vocab: [冬休み](冬休み.md) 

## Mnemonics:
お前が今までの中では一番の彼女だよ。
* You are the best girlfriend I’ve had so far.
* You know that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [今](https://jisho.org/search/今)</span> means now. What you probably don't know is that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [まで](https://jisho.org/search/まで)</span> means until. So, this word means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> until now</span>.
* The reading for this word is the kun'yomi reading which you learned previously when you learned the vocab word <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [今](https://jisho.org/search/今)</span>. You can tell this is a kun'yomi reading because it has hiragana attached. So if you know that, you should be able to read this word as well.
