## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> drift</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to drift</span>.
* You're going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to drift</span> away. Drifting drifting drifting. Then, you turn into a yo-yo and yell <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ta da yo</span>-yo (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ただよ](https://jisho.org/search/ただよ)</span>)! Not sure why drifting turns you into a yo-yo, but imagine it and don't question it.
