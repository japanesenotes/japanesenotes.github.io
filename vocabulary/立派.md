## Mnemonics:

* 
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sect</span> that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> stand</span>s the highest are those who are <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> high class</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> splendid</span>, and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> fine</span>. <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [立派だ](https://jisho.org/search/立派だ)</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [りつ](https://jisho.org/search/りつ)</span> shortens to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [りっ](https://jisho.org/search/りっ)</span> though, so keep an eye on that you splendid frood you. Oh and <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [は](https://jisho.org/search/は)</span> changes to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぱ](https://jisho.org/search/ぱ)</span> too. Because rendaku and all that.
