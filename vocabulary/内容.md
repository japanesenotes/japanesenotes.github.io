## Mnemonics:
一部のTofuguファンは、内容と正確さについて非常に厳しいことでよく知られています。
* Some of Tofugu's fans are well known for being relentless about substance and accuracy.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Inside</span> that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> form</span>... there is something inside there. What are the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> contents</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
