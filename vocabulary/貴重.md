## Mnemonics:

* 
* Something that's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> valuable</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> heavy</span> is extremely <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> precious</span> and probably even more <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> valuable</span> than your less heavy things.
* This is a jukugo word that uses the on'yomi readings of the kanji. You didn't learn this reading for <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [重](https://jisho.org/search/重)</span> though, so here's a mnemonic to help you:<br />Keep things <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> precious</span> to you very close, because the evil Mrs. <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Chou</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ちょう](https://jisho.org/search/ちょう)</span>) will come and steal it away if you don't.
