# <bigfont> 彩る</bigfont> - To Color Something, To Color 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 彩る | To Color Something, To Color | いろどる |  [彩](../kanjis/彩.md) (Coloring, Colouring) | [Level 48](../levels/wk_level48.md) |

Previous vocab: [尽力](尽力.md) Next vocab: [色彩](色彩.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> coloring</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to color something</span>, because you're doing coloring.
* You go <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to color something</span>, painting the coloring all over the wall. Problem is, the coloring is made of ACID. This causes the wall to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> erode</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いろど](https://jisho.org/search/いろど)</span>).
