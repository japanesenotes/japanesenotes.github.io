## Mnemonics:

* 
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> not</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sleep</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> symptom</span> shows you have a disorder that involves not sleeping. That is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> insomnia</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 不, 眠, 症
