| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [開始](開始.md) Next vocab: [表す](表す.md) 

## Mnemonics:
明日、みんなの前でSNSの研究成果について発表をしなければならないので、緊張しています。
* I'm nervous because I have to give a presentation about my social media research outcome in front of everyone tomorrow.
* I'm going to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> express</span> my <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> departure</span>. That is my only <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> announcement</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ひょう](https://jisho.org/search/ひょう)</span> changes to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぴょう](https://jisho.org/search/ぴょう)</span> and the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はつ](https://jisho.org/search/はつ)</span> gets shortened, though, so just about everything about this word is weird.
