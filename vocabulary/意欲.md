## Mnemonics:

* 
* If you have an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> idea</span> of what you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> want</span> already, then that's good! You have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> will</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> desire</span>, and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ambition</span>. Those who don't know what they want can't have any of these.
