## Mnemonics:
仏典がどのようにしてサンスクリット語から漢語に訳されたのかご存じですか？
* Do you know how the Buddhist scriptures were translated from Sanskrit to Chinese?
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Buddha</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> rule</span>s are the rules Buddha made for Buddhists. These can be found in the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Buddhist Scriptures</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Buddhist Writing</span>s.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. Note that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぶつ](https://jisho.org/search/ぶつ)</span> gets shortened to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぶっ](https://jisho.org/search/ぶっ)</span>.
