# <bigfont> 急ぐ</bigfont> - To Hurry 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 急ぐ | To Hurry | いそぐ |  [急](../kanjis/急.md) (Hurry) | [Level 16](../levels/wk_level16.md) |

Previous vocab: [〜弁](〜弁.md) Next vocab: [急に](急に.md) 

## Mnemonics:
「もう！急いでよ！遅れちゃうわ。」「今起きたから、すぐ行くわ。」
* Hey, hurry up! We’ll be late. I just woke up, I’m coming.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hurry</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to hurry</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: Someone who is trying <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to hurry</span> is someone who is not at <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ease</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いそ](https://jisho.org/search/いそ)</span>).
