## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> loose</span>, so the verb version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to loosen</span>. This is when something loosens on its own, and not via your actions. You know this because a cow is loosening things, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> moo</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [む](https://jisho.org/search/む)</span>)...
* The reading is the same as the one you learned with the kanji!
