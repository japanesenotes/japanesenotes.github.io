## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> obsolete</span> and this word means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to become obsolete</span>.
* Everything has <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to become obsolete</span>. Even the biggest most fiery things, like a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sta</span>r, for example, will go obsolete at some point.
