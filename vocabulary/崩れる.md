## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> crumble</span> and the verb vocab version means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to crumble</span>, like, something is crumbling or collapsing.
* The reading comes from <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [崩す](https://jisho.org/search/崩す)</span>, so if you know that you know this reading as well!
