| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 入力 | Input, Enter, Entry | にゅうりょく |  [入](../kanjis/入.md) (Enter), [力](../kanjis/力.md) (Power, Strength) | [Level 2](../levels/wk_level2.md) |

## Mnemonics:
「ローマじ入力」と「かな入力」、どちらをつかっていますか？
* Do you use romaji input or kana input?
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Enter</span> something with all your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> strength</span> and you will look down to see that what you're doing is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> input</span>ting data. You're inputting it right now! Into this site! The answers you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> enter</span> into WaniKani with all your strength can be said with this word. Wow. Full circle already, and you're only on level 2. Look at you go!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 入, 力
* Enter, Power
