## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> pluck</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to pluck</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to pick</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to prune</span>, like with plants.
* You are going outside <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to pluck</span> some <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> tu</span>lips (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つ](https://jisho.org/search/つ)</span>).
