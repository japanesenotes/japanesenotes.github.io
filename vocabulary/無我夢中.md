# <bigfont> 無我夢中</bigfont> - Total Absorption, Ecstasy 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 無我夢中 | Total Absorption, Ecstasy | むがむちゅう |  [無](../kanjis/無.md) (Nothing), [我](../kanjis/我.md) (I, Me), [夢](../kanjis/夢.md) (Dream), [中](../kanjis/中.md) (Middle, In, Inside, Center) | [Level 28](../levels/wk_level28.md) |

Previous vocab: [憲政](憲政.md) Next vocab: [身振り](身振り.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [夢中](https://jisho.org/search/夢中)</span> is crazy about. So, if <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> I</span> (talking about you here) am <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nothing</span> when put next to this thing you're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> crazy about</span>, then you are really, really into it. You have become nothing, at least when you compare yourself to it. This is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> total absorption</span> (though it can also be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ecstasy</span>).
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 無, 我, 夢, 中
* Nothing, I, Dream, Middle
