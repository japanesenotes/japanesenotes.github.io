## Mnemonics:
Tofuguソフトボールチームに入る際の第二規則は、お腹がすきすぎちゃうので、昼食前には「ベーコン」という言葉は禁句だということを肝に銘じることです。
* The second rule of Tofugu’s softball team is that you have to keep in mind that bacon is a taboo word before lunch time because it makes us too hungry.
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> paragraph</span> that is under <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> prohibition</span> is under prohibition because it contains a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> taboo word</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> taboo phrase</span>. Do not say this word, please!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 禁, 句
* Prohibit, Paragraph
