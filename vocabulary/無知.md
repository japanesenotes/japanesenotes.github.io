# <bigfont> 無知</bigfont> - Ignorance, Ignorant 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 無知 | Ignorance, Ignorant | むち |  [無](../kanjis/無.md) (Nothing), [知](../kanjis/知.md) (Know) | [Level 17](../levels/wk_level17.md) |

Previous vocab: [無事](無事.md) Next vocab: [無茶](無茶.md) 

## Mnemonics:
無知をさらけ出したからって恥ずかしがることなんか無いよ。あいつらこそ、自分たちの無知を隠していることを恥じるべきさ。
* You shouldn’t be ashamed of yourself after admitting your ignorance. They should be ashamed of themselves for hiding their own ignorance.
* When you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> know</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nothing</span> (Jon Snow), you know nothing. This is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ignorance</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 無, 知
* Nothing, Know
