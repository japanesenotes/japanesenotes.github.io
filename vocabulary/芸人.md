| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 芸人 | Comedian, Performer, Entertainer | げいにん |  [芸](../kanjis/芸.md) (Acting, Art), [人](../kanjis/人.md) (Person) | [Level 14](../levels/wk_level14.md) |

Previous vocab: [芸能界](芸能界.md) Next vocab: [芸者](芸者.md) 

## Mnemonics:
彼は根っからの芸人かもしれない。
* He might be a natural-born performer.
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> person</span> who does humorous <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> acting</span> is a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> comedian</span>. You might think that this would be actor, but it's usually a comedian. Just think of them like more of a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> performer</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 芸, 人
* Acting, Person
