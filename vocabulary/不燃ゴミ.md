## Mnemonics:

* 
* These are <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> not</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> burn</span>able garbages (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ごみ](https://jisho.org/search/ごみ)</span>). Pay attention to this on signs when you throw things away. You want to put the right things into <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nonburnable garbage</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 不, 燃
* Not, Burn
