## Mnemonics:
最近、嬉しいことが全然ない。
* Nothing happy has happened to me lately.
* This is a single kanji with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [い](https://jisho.org/search/い)</span> on the end, meaning you know it's probably an adjective. What's the adjective form of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> glad</span>? It's also <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> glad</span>!
* Whenever you're really <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> glad</span> to be alive, you look up at the sun's rays and sing, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Ooh, ray</span>s (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [うれ](https://jisho.org/search/うれ)</span>), I'm so glad you're here! Then your Uncle Ray comes dancing into the room and sings back, Ooh, Ray is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> happy</span> you're happy! The sun's rays and Uncle Ray both make you really happy.
