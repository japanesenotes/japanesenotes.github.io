## Mnemonics:
2008年の世界的な金融危機では、Tofuguも経営難に陥ったのですか？
* Did Tofugu run into trouble during the global financial crisis of 2008?
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dangerous</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> machine</span> is walking through your town, destroying everything. This is a huge <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> crisis</span>!
