## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> steal</span>, so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to steal</span>.
* What are you going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to steal</span>? How about a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> noose</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぬす](https://jisho.org/search/ぬす)</span>)? Wait, why are you stealing a noose? That sounds like trouble to me...
