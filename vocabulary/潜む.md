## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conceal</span>, so if you conceal yourself and walk around you are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to lurk</span> or you are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to lie dormant</span>.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> He so</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ひそ](https://jisho.org/search/ひそ)</span>) hidden. Where you at?
