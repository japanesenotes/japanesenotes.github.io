| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 耐える | To Endure, To Withstand | たえる |  [耐](../kanjis/耐.md) (Resistant) | [Level 48](../levels/wk_level48.md) |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> resistant</span>. If you're resisting things, then you're enduring and withstanding them. That's why this is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to endure</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to withstand</span>.
* What is it that you have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to endure</span>? A barrage of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ta</span>cos (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [た](https://jisho.org/search/た)</span>), coming at you.
* 耐
