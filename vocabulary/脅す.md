* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. * I'm going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to threaten</span> you with your life if you have a bad <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> odo</span>r (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [おど](https://jisho.org/search/おど)</span>). Imagine someone with a bad odor, then threaten them.
