## Mnemonics:
おい、お前って本当に阿呆だな。ダーウィンの進化論はディズニー映画のタイトルなんかじゃねえよ。
* Jeez, you’re really dumb. Darwin’s theory of evolution is not the title of a Disney movie.
* Think of things that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> advance</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> change</span>. Does this sound familiar? Does this sound like <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> evolution</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.

* Advance, Change
