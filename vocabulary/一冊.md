| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 一冊 | One Book, One Volume | いっさつ |  [一](../kanjis/一.md) (One), [冊](../kanjis/冊.md) (Book Counter, Counter For Books, Counter For Volumes) | [Level 19](../levels/wk_level19.md) |

Previous vocab: [八冊](八冊.md) Next vocab: [別冊](別冊.md) 

## Mnemonics:
あなたのために旅の間に読める本を一冊買っておいたの。タイトルは『地図』よ。
* I bought you a book to read during your trip. The title is Map.
* When you have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> one</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> books</span> you really just have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> one book</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [一](https://jisho.org/search/一)</span> portion gets shortened to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いっ](https://jisho.org/search/いっ)</span> as it often is.
* 一, 冊
* One, Book Counter
