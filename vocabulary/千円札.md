## Mnemonics:
「千ドル札を燃やしてるの？」「いや、ただの千円札だよ。」
* Are you burning a thousand dollar bill? No, it’s just a thousand yen bill.
* You know what <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [千円](https://jisho.org/search/千円)</span> is (1000 yen). So if you add a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bill</span> to that you get a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> thousand yen bill</span>. That's just the little rectangular piece of money that is worth 1000 yen.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 千, 円, 札
* Thousand, Yen, Bill
