# <bigfont> 精神病</bigfont> - Mental Illness, Mental Disorder 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 精神病 | Mental Illness, Mental Disorder | せいしんびょう |  [精](../kanjis/精.md) (Spirit, Pure), [神](../kanjis/神.md) (God), [病](../kanjis/病.md) (Sick, Sickness, Ill, Illness) | [Level 30](../levels/wk_level30.md) |

Previous vocab: [精神的](精神的.md) Next vocab: [〜系](〜系.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [精神](https://jisho.org/search/精神)</span> is your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> spirit</span>, both religiously and spiritually. When those are <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sick</span>, you have a problem. Back in the day, a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mental illness</span> was thought to be a sickness with your spirit. Nowadays you can just call it a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mental illness</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mental disorder</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. Will help if you know <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [精神](https://jisho.org/search/精神)</span> already, though.
* 精, 神, 病
* Spirit, God, Sick
