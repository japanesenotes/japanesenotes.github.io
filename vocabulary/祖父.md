# <bigfont> 祖父</bigfont> - Grandfather, Grandpa 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 祖父 | Grandfather, Grandpa | そふ |  [祖](../kanjis/祖.md) (Ancestor), [父](../kanjis/父.md) (Father, Dad) | [Level 32](../levels/wk_level32.md) |

Previous vocab: [先祖](先祖.md) Next vocab: [祖母](祖母.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ancestor</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> father</span> isn't your father, it's the father that came before your father's generation, your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> grandfather</span>.
* The reading is all on'yomi, which is great for <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [祖](https://jisho.org/search/祖)</span> but not as great for <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [父](https://jisho.org/search/父)</span>. To remember that this <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [父](https://jisho.org/search/父)</span> is <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ふ](https://jisho.org/search/ふ)</span>, just think about how much of a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> foo</span>l (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ふ](https://jisho.org/search/ふ)</span>) your grandfather was, even if he wasn't.
* 祖, 父
* Ancestor, Father
