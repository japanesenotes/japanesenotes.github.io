## Mnemonics:
お昼ごはん、食堂で食べない？もしよければ、私二限目のクラスが無いから、席取っておけるよ。
* Do you want to eat lunch in the cafeteria? If so, I don’t have a second period class, so I could find some seats for us.
* An <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> eat</span>ing <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hall</span> is a big room in which you eat. What big rooms do you eat in? How about a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cafeteria</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dining hall</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 食, 堂
* Eat, Hall
