# <bigfont> 粘る</bigfont> - To Be Sticky, To Persevere 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 粘る | To Be Sticky, To Persevere | ねばる |  [粘](../kanjis/粘.md) (Sticky) | [Level 47](../levels/wk_level47.md) |

Previous vocab: [粉砕](粉砕.md) Next vocab: [粘土](粘土.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sticky</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be sticky</span>. It can also mean <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to persevere</span>, because if you're being sticky towards the situation, not giving up and not letting go. You're totally persevering by sticking around.
* Will I give up? I'll <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nevah</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ねば](https://jisho.org/search/ねば)</span>) give up! I'll also <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nevah</span> unstick from you.
