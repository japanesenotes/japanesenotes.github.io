## Mnemonics:
空こうで、まやくをかくしもっていないか、身体けんさをされました。
* At the airport, they searched me for hidden drugs.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Somebody</span>'s <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> body</span>. This refers to the very general word <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> the body</span>. As in, the human body or something like that. This can also refer to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> health</span> as well.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
