## Mnemonics:
豆腐屋さんの朝はいつも早い。
* A tofu maker always wakes up early in the morning.
* This is a single kanji with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [い](https://jisho.org/search/い)</span> on the end, meaning you know it's probably an adjective. What's the adjective form of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> early</span>? It's also <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> early</span>.
* The reading for this word is the kun'yomi reading, which you did not learn when you learned the kanji. You have to remember the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はや](https://jisho.org/search/はや)</span> portion to be able read this word. In order to do that, here's a mnemonic: <br />You're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> fast</span>! You're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> early</span>! Your chop comes down onto your opponent's neck so quickly! As you chop him, you yell <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> はや</span>!
