## Mnemonics:
日本には、電子公告規則という法律がありますが、あなたの国はどうですか？
* There is a law in Japan called the Electronic Public Notice Regulation, do you have anything like that in your country?
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> public</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> announcement</span> is a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> public notice</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
