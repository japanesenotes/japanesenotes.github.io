## Mnemonics:

* 
* You have <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [時代](https://jisho.org/search/時代)</span> as a period of time you're talking about. What period? The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> infancy</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> year</span>s. Those years are not just when you're a baby, but when you were growing up, too. That was your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> childhood</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 幼, 年, 時, 代
* Infancy, Year, Time, Substitute
