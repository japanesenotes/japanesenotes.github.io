# <bigfont> 失敗</bigfont> - Failure, Mistake 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 失敗 | Failure, Mistake | しっぱい |  [失](../kanjis/失.md) (Fault), [敗](../kanjis/敗.md) (Failure, Fail) | [Level 17](../levels/wk_level17.md) |

Previous vocab: [敗れる](敗れる.md) Next vocab: [勝敗](勝敗.md) 

## Mnemonics:
正直言って、君が作ったこの映画は失敗作だね。でも、一度失敗したからといって、くよくよしてちゃ駄目だよ。誰かも、「成功とは、失敗から失敗へと熱意を失うことなく進んでいける能力である」って言ってただろ？
* To be honest, this movie you made is a flop. However, you can’t let it get you down just because you failed once. Someone said that success is the ability to go from one failure to another with no loss of enthusiasm, right?
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> fault</span> and a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> failure</span>, all in one. This must have been one big <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> failure</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mistake</span> you've made.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [失](https://jisho.org/search/失)</span> gets shortened, though. Don't make that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [失](https://jisho.org/search/失)敗</span>.
* 失, 敗
* Fault, Failure
