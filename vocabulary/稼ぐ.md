# <bigfont> 稼ぐ</bigfont> - To Earn Income, To Make Money 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 稼ぐ | To Earn Income, To Make Money | かせぐ |  [稼](../kanjis/稼.md) (Earnings) | [Level 45](../levels/wk_level45.md) |

Previous vocab: [硬直](硬直.md) Next vocab: [澄む](澄む.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> earnings</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to earn income</span>, because you can't to income something. Can also be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to make money</span>.
* You earn your income, and every time you're paid, you're given your money in a brief<span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> case</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かせ](https://jisho.org/search/かせ)</span>). Imagine getting your income from a briefcase, opening it up, and grabbing the money from within. Then, you have to give the briefcase back at the specified dropoff.
