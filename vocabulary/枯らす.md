# <bigfont> 枯らす</bigfont> - To Let Dry, To Kill, To Season 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 枯らす | To Let Dry, To Kill, To Season | からす |  [枯](../kanjis/枯.md) (Wither) | [Level 53](../levels/wk_level53.md) |

Previous vocab: [枯れ木](枯れ木.md) Next vocab: [沸かす](沸かす.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* You already learned the word <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [枯れる](https://jisho.org/search/枯れる)</span> which means to wither, but this is when you are the one making a plant wither. It means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to let dry</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to kill</span> your plants. They didn't just die on their own, you did it.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You already learned this reading when you learned <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [枯れる](https://jisho.org/search/枯れる)</span>, so you should be able to read this word as well.
* 枯
