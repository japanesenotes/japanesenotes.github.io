## Mnemonics:
インドには多様な民族がいますが、みんながみんなカレーを食べるのでしょうか。
* India has a lot of ethnic diversity but I wonder if all of them still eat curry.
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> peoples</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> tribe</span> (or tribe of peoples) is a group that all stick together. They are one single <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ethnic group</span>. What's your <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [民族](https://jisho.org/search/民族)</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 民, 族
* Peoples, Tribe
