# <bigfont> 炊く</bigfont> - To Cook, To Boil, To Cook Grains 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 炊く | To Cook, To Boil, To Cook Grains | たく |  [炊](../kanjis/炊.md) (Cook) | [Level 48](../levels/wk_level48.md) |

Previous vocab: [点滴](点滴.md) Next vocab: [自炊](自炊.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cook</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to cook</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to boil</span>. Often it has to do with cooking grains (<span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to cook grains</span>).
* When you cook something, it's like you're a<span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ttack</span>ing (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [たく](https://jisho.org/search/たく)</span>) the food with boiling water or fire or whatever. Attack the food by cooking it!
