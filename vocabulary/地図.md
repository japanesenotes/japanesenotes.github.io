## Mnemonics:
先生、もし世界地図を忘れたのなら、私の額にタトゥーの世界地図がありますよ。
* Teacher, if you forget your world map I have one tattoo'd on my forehead.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> diagram</span> of the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> earth</span>. What could that be other than a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> map</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 地, 図
* Earth, Diagram
