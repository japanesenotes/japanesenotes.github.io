## Mnemonics:
今に見てなさい。あのフグ少年はきっと面倒を引き起こすわよ。
* Mark my words. That Fugu boy is going to cause trouble.
* When you're looking straight into the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> face</span> of a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> collapse</span> it means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> trouble</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> difficulty</span> coming soon.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [とう](https://jisho.org/search/とう)</span> goes to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [どう](https://jisho.org/search/どう)</span>, though. Agh, what a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [面倒](https://jisho.org/search/面倒)</span></span>.
