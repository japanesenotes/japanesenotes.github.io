## Mnemonics:
いつも右の靴の踵が左よりも速く擦り減るんだ。
* My right shoe always gets worn down at the heels faster than my left one.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> decrease</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be decreased</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: Your status is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be decreased</span> amongst the eyes of the Gods. They decide your punishment is to be decreased into <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hell</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [へる](https://jisho.org/search/へる)</span>) where you will live the rest of your days.
