| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> inferiority</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be inferior to</span>.
* When thinking about cars, an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> auto</span>matic (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [おと](https://jisho.org/search/おと)</span>) car is inferior to a manual car. Sure, manual is harder, but way more interesting to drive.
* 劣
* Inferiority
