Previous vocab: [穴子](穴子.md) Next vocab: [経験](経験.md) 

## Mnemonics:
「あいつら、幸せな結婚生活を送れると思う？」「時間が経てばわかるよ。」
* Do you think they will have a happy marriage? Time will tell.
* You know how <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [経](https://jisho.org/search/経)</span> means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> passage of time</span>, right? Well, this is the verb form of that. What does time do when it passages? It passes. So, this verb means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to pass</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: What do you say to time as it begins <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to pass</span> you by? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Ta</span> ta (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [た](https://jisho.org/search/た)</span>), Time!
