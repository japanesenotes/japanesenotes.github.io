## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> leap</span> so the verb vocab version means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to leap</span>. It also means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to jump</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to bounce</span>.
* You go <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to leap</span> and leap again. You do it so much that you start sweating, which is causing a foul <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> odo</span>r (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [おど](https://jisho.org/search/おど)</span>) to come from your body.
