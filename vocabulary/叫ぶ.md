## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shout</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to shout</span>. Can also be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to scream</span>.
* To remember the reading of this, just think about how you start <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to shout</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to scream</span> when you drink way too much <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sake</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [さけ](https://jisho.org/search/さけ)</span>).
