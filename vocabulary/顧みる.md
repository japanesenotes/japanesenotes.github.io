# <bigfont> 顧みる</bigfont> - To Look Back On, To Reflect On

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [顧問](顧問.md) Next vocab: [回顧録](回顧録.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> review</span>. What do you do when you review something? You look back on it. Meaning <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to look back on</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to reflect on</span>.
* For the reading, think about another word that is similar: <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [帰る](https://jisho.org/search/帰る)</span>. With <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [帰る](https://jisho.org/search/帰る)</span> you are returning to somewhere. With this word, you're returning to look back on something. They share the same first part reading, so you should take advantage of that. <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [帰る](https://jisho.org/search/帰る)</span> → <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かえり](https://jisho.org/search/かえり)</span>.
