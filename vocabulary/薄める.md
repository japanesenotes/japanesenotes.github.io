| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dilute</span> and the verb vocab version means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to dilute</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to water down</span>.
* The reading comes from <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [薄い](https://jisho.org/search/薄い)</span>, so if you know that you'll be able to read this too.
* 薄
