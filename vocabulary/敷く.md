## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> spread</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to spread out</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to lay out</span>.
* What is getting <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> spread out</span>? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Shee</span>p (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [し](https://jisho.org/search/し)</span>)! Someone's been hunting sheep, because you're looking at a bunch of empty (dead) sheep bodies spread out on the ground.
