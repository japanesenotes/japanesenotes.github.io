## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hate</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to hate</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to detest</span>.
* What do you hate and detest more than anything? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> 肉</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [にく](https://jisho.org/search/にく)</span>). That stuff is gross, and you detest it.
