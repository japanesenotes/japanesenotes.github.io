## Mnemonics:
どうして人々は宇宙人が地球にやってくる事を妨げようとするのか理解できません。
* I don’t understand why people try preventing aliens from reaching the earth.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> obstruct</span>, so what is the verb vocab version? It's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to obstruct</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: Your woman friend (see radical) is trying <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to obstruct</span> you. <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Sama(n)tha</span>! (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [さまた](https://jisho.org/search/さまた)</span>) you yell. Let me pass!
