# <bigfont> 毎日</bigfont> - Every Day, Daily 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 毎日 | Every Day, Daily | まいにち |  [毎](../kanjis/毎.md) (Every, Each), [日](../kanjis/日.md) (Sun, Day) | [Level 5](../levels/wk_level5.md) |

Previous vocab: [毎月](毎月.md) Next vocab: [毎年](毎年.md) 

## Mnemonics:
毎日17から21個のチーズバーガーを食べます。
* I eat anywhere between 17-21 cheeseburgers every day.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Every</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> day</span> is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> daily</span> (or just <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> every day</span>).
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 毎, 日
* Every, Sun
