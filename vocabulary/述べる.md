## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mention</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to mention</span>.
* I have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to mention</span> this thing... <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> NO</span>, (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [の](https://jisho.org/search/の)</span>) you quickly respond. I don't want you mentioning that here and now.
