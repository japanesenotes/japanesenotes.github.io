| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [求人](求人.md) Next vocab: [料理](料理.md) 

## Mnemonics:
サーモンと彼女に翻弄される人々の心理を、君の小説で描いてみてもらえないかな？人気が出ると思うんだけど。
* Can you depict the mental state of Salmon and those she deceives in your novel? I think people would love it.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> reason</span>ing that your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> heart</span> makes decides what you do. Probably when kanji was being made people thought that the heart directed our actions, not our brains. So, if you think about it that way, the reasoning of your heart is your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mental state</span>. Nowadays that's known as <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> psychology</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 心, 理
* Heart, Reason
