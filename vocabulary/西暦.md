## Mnemonics:

* 
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> West</span>'s <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> calendar</span> shows the history and time period of The West. To the Japanese and Chinese, this era is known as the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Christian Era</span>. It also used to be used as a way to refer to the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Western Calendar</span>, though we usually just say <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [カレンダー](https://jisho.org/search/カレンダー)</span> nowadays.
* This is a jukugo word that uses the on'yomi readings of the kanji. You should be able to read this on your own.
* 西, 暦
* West, Calendar
