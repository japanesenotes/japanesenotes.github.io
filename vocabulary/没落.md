## Mnemonics:

* 
* A building or civilization that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> die</span>s and then <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> falls</span> is in a state of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ruin</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> collapse</span>. Like the Mayans or the Western Roman Empire. They fell pretty hard.
* This is a jukugo word that uses the on'yomi readings of the kanji. You should be able to read this on your own.
