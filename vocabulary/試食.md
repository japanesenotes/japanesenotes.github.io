## Mnemonics:
スーパーで試食販売をしている人は、日本の食品業界では「マネキンさん」と呼ばれる。
* The person who offers food samples in grocery stores is called Ms. Mannequin in the Japanese food industry.
* Go ahead and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> try</span> an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> eat</span>. How was that little try? That little <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sample</span>? That little <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> taste</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 試, 食
* Try, Eat
