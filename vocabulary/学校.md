# <bigfont> 学校</bigfont> - School 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 学校 | School | がっこう |  [学](../kanjis/学.md) (Study, Learn, Learning), [校](../kanjis/校.md) (School) | [Level 7](../levels/wk_level7.md) |

Previous vocab: [高校](高校.md) Next vocab: [夏](夏.md) 

## Mnemonics:
今日はどうして学校で授業中にホットドッグをバーベキューするの？
* Why are we barbecuing hot dogs at school during class today?
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> study</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> school</span> is obviously a place where people study and learn. In English, that's simply a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> school</span>.
* This jukugo word consists of on'yomi readings, except <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [学](https://jisho.org/search/学)</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [がく](https://jisho.org/search/がく)</span>) is shortened with a small <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [っ](https://jisho.org/search/っ)</span> to make it <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> がっこう</span>.
* 学, 校
