| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 理論 | Theory | りろん |  [理](../kanjis/理.md) (Reason), [論](../kanjis/論.md) (Theory) | [Level 20](../levels/wk_level20.md) |

Previous vocab: [論文](論文.md) Next vocab: [論理](論理.md) 

## Mnemonics:
理論的に説明するのは苦手です。
* I’m bad at giving theoretical explanations.
* You have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> reason</span> behind your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> theory</span>, making it a real <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> theory</span> (unlike those that don't have reason, I guess?).
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 理, 論
* Reason, Theory
