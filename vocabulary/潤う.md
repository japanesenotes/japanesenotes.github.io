# <bigfont> 潤う</bigfont> - To Be Moist, To Be Damp, To Be Watered 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 潤う | To Be Moist, To Be Damp, To Be Watered | うるおう |  [潤](../kanjis/潤.md) (Watered) | [Level 49](../levels/wk_level49.md) |

Previous vocab: [桑畑](桑畑.md) Next vocab: [利潤](利潤.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> watered</span>. When something is watered, it is moist and damp. That's why this verb is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be moist</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be damp</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be watered</span>.
* If you get moist and damp, then <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> you're all</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)るお</span>) wet. It's just how it is.
* 潤
* Watered
