## Mnemonics:
コウイチとビエトが乗り込むと、そのタクシーは静かに動き出した。
* When Koichi and Viet got in, the taxi quietly started to move.
* This is the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [な](https://jisho.org/search/な)</span> adjective form of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> quiet</span>, which is kind of just like the noun, anyways. What is the adjective version of quiet? It's also <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> quiet</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: You need to be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> quiet</span>. <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> She's</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [しず](https://jisho.org/search/しず)</span>) being nice and quiet, unlike you!
