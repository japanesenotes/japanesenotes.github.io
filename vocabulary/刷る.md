## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> printing</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to print</span>.
* When you print something out, it's usually the paperwork needed in order to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sue</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [す](https://jisho.org/search/す)</span>) someone. You're just that kind of person, always looking to sue.
