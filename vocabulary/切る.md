## Mnemonics:
うっかりかみのけをみじかく切りすぎちゃったけど、そのあといぜんよりもよくナンパされるようになった。
* Even though I accidentally cut my hair shorter than I intended, more people have been flirting with me since.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cut</span>, and the verb form of that is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to cut</span>.
* When you think <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to cut</span> something, what do you think about cutting? One thing is cutting down a tree, which is also known as <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [木](https://jisho.org/search/木)</span>, or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> き</span>. You <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [きる]([き](https://jisho.org/search/き)る)</span> a <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [き](https://jisho.org/search/き)</span>.
