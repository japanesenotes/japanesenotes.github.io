## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> challenge</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to challenge someone</span>.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> I do</span>n't (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いど](https://jisho.org/search/いど)</span>) just challenge anyone, but when I challenge someone, it's for real. My challenging reflexes come from my <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Id</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いど](https://jisho.org/search/いど)</span>), after all.
