## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> rot</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to rot</span>.
* Remember the word <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [臭い](https://jisho.org/search/臭い)</span>, which means stinky? When something rots, it becomes stinky, and all <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> くさい</span>.
