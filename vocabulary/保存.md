| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 保存 | Preservation, Conservation, Saving | ほぞん |  [保](../kanjis/保.md) (Preserve, Guarantee), [存](../kanjis/存.md) (Exist, Suppose) | [Level 16](../levels/wk_level16.md) |

Previous vocab: [保守的](保守的.md) Next vocab: [保持](保持.md) 

## Mnemonics:
「このベーコンは、着色保存料を一切使用しておりません。」「ってことは、本当のベーコンではありませんね。」
* This bacon contains no food coloring or preservatives. Well, then it really isn’t bacon.
* We try to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> preserve</span> anything we want to <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> exist</span> with <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> preservation</span>. Want a special sea creature or bird to exist? Up <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conservation</span> efforts! And, if you want to save data (like on a computer), you can use this word for that kind of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> saving</span>, as well.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 保, 存
* Preserve, Exist
