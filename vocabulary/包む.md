## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> wrap</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to wrap</span>.
* You never wrap things once, you always wrap them twice. And then you double that. It's a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> two two</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つつ](https://jisho.org/search/つつ)</span>) wrapping technique.
