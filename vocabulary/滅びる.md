## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> destroy</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be destroyed</span>. You know you're not destroying things because of the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> beer</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [びる](https://jisho.org/search/びる)</span>). You'd never destroy beer... but someone else might. Grrr.
* THE <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> HORROR</span>! (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ほろ](https://jisho.org/search/ほろ)</span>) you cry, as your beer is about <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to be destroyed</span>.
* 滅
