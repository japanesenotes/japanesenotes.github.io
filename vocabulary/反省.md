## Mnemonics:

* 
* If you're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> anti</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conserve</span>, I'm going to ask you to do some heavy <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> reconsideration</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> reflection</span>, and see if that's really the right way to be. Think long and hard, okay?
* The reading for <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [反](https://jisho.org/search/反)</span> is all good. The reading for <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [省](https://jisho.org/search/省)</span> is an on'yomi reading you haven't seen yet. Here's a mnemonic:<br />I'm standing in front of you, forcing a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> reconsideration</span> of your anti conserve stance, with my <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sa</span>ber (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [せい](https://jisho.org/search/せい)</span>) to your throat. I better see some reflection on your face or you won't have one in a second.
