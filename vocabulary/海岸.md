# <bigfont> 海岸</bigfont> - Seashore, Coast, Seacoast 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 海岸 | Seashore, Coast, Seacoast | かいがん |  [海](../kanjis/海.md) (Sea, Ocean), [岸](../kanjis/岸.md) (Coast, Shore) | [Level 11](../levels/wk_level11.md) |

## Mnemonics:
「今日、私達と一緒に海岸に行かない？」「またの機会にするよ。」
* Do you want to go to the seashore with us today? I’ll take a rain check this time.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> ocean</span>/<span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sea</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> coast</span> is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> seashore</span>. Then again, it could also be the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> coast</span> as well.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 海, 岸
* Sea, Coast
