| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [憶測](憶測.md) Next vocab: [溶岩](溶岩.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> melt</span> and this word means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to melt something</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to melt</span>. You're doing the melting, and you know it because you are the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cause</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [かす](https://jisho.org/search/かす)</span>) of the melting.
* The thing that you melted in this situation is your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> toe</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [と](https://jisho.org/search/と)</span>). Feel your toe getting melted away. Augghh it hurts.
* 溶
* Melt
