# <bigfont> 不明</bigfont> - Unknown, Unclear, Uncertain, Unsure 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 不明 | Unknown, Unclear, Uncertain, Unsure | ふめい |  [不](../kanjis/不.md) (Not), [明](../kanjis/明.md) (Bright) | [Level 6](../levels/wk_level6.md) |

Previous vocab: [明るい](明るい.md) Next vocab: [東方](東方.md) 

## Mnemonics:
え？寝る時、玉ねぎを枕にしたの？あんたの言ってること、意味不明なんだけど。
* Huh? When you were sleeping you used an onion for a pillow? I don’t really get what you’re saying.
* Something that is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> not</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bright</span> is cloudy and fuzzy. What do you think when you look into the fuzzy clouds? It is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> unknown</span>, the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> unclear</span>, the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> uncertain</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
