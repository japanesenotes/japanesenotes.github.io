## Mnemonics:
減給になっちゃって泣いてます。もっと給料のいい会社に移りたいよ。
* I’m crying because my salary was cut. I want to transfer to a higher paying job.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cry</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to cry</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. If you remember the kanji reading you'll have no problem with this word.
