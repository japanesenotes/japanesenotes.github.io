# <bigfont> 人込み</bigfont> - Crowd Of People, Crowded, Crowded With People 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 人込み | Crowd Of People, Crowded, Crowded With People | ひとごみ |  [人](../kanjis/人.md) (Person), [込](../kanjis/込.md) (Crowded) | [Level 34](../levels/wk_level34.md) |

Previous vocab: [書き込む](書き込む.md) Next vocab: [染み](染み.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* You know that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [込む](https://jisho.org/search/込む)</span> means to be crowded so when you are crowded with people... well... you have a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> crowd of people</span>.
* The reading comes from <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [人](https://jisho.org/search/人)</span> and <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [込む](https://jisho.org/search/込む)</span> combined together into one. Just keep in mind that the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こ](https://jisho.org/search/こ)</span> from <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [込み](https://jisho.org/search/込み)</span> changes to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ご](https://jisho.org/search/ご)</span> and you'll be fine.
* 人, 込
* Person, Crowded
