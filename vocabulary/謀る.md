## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conspire</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to conspire</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to plot</span>.
* A group of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hacker</span>s (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はか](https://jisho.org/search/はか)</span>) are getting together and trying to conspire and plot to take down the mainframe! Those hackers!
