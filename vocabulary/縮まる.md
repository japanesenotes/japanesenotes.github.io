## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> shrink</span> so the verb vocab version means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to shrink</span>.
* Jesus couldn't cause the monster <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to shrink</span>. He had to transform into <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cheese jesus</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ちぢ](https://jisho.org/search/ちぢ)</span>), a more powerful version of himself, in order to shrink the monster down to size.
* 縮
