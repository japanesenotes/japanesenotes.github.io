## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> resemble</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to resemble</span>.
* What are you trying <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to resemble</span>?? Is that... oh no. A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> knee</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [に](https://jisho.org/search/に)</span>) face resemble surgery, where you make your face look like your knee and vice versa? Do you know how dangerous that surgery is?
