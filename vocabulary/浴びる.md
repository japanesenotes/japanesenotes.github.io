## Mnemonics:
フグは私に親友の天然鯛を紹介してくれました。鯛君は、深海に住んでいるので日光をあまり浴びないんだと言っていました。
* Fugu introduced me his best friend, Wild Red Sea Bream. He told me Sea Bream lives at great depths and is bathed in hardly any sunlight.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bathe</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to bathe</span>.
* You want to know the best way <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to bathe</span>? With a bunch of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> a</span>ardvarks (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [あ](https://jisho.org/search/あ)</span>) in the water. They use their sticky little tongues to clean all your cracks and crannies.
