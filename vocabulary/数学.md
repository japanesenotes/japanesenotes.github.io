## Mnemonics:
数学さんよ、どうか大人になって自分の問題は自分で解決してくれよな。
* Dear Math, please grow up and solve your own problems by yourself.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> study</span> of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> number</span>s is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mathematics</span> (or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> math</span>).
