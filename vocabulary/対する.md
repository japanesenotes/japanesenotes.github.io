# <bigfont> 対する</bigfont> - To Compare With, To Face Each Other, To Oppose, To Compare, To Contrast, To Contrast With 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 対する | To Compare With, To Face Each Other, To Oppose, To Compare, To Contrast, To Contrast With | たいする |  [対](../kanjis/対.md) (Versus, Opposite) | [Level 8](../levels/wk_level8.md) |

Previous vocab: [〜君](〜君.md) Next vocab: [対外](対外.md) 

## Mnemonics:
あいつらの関係が修復不可能だってことは明らかだね。だって、スタートレックに対する意見が全く違っているんだから。
* It’s clear now that their relationship will never work because their opinions on Star Trek are the complete opposite.
* When you do (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [する](https://jisho.org/search/する)</span>) <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> versus</span> you're putting two things together <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to compare with</span> each other. Imagine putting two dogs in a ring (show dogs, not fighting dogs, c'mon). Then someone announces the start, and the judges look at them, trying to compare one to the other to decide which is better.<br />Alternatively, you can use this to say <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to face each other</span> as well.
* The reading is the same as the one you learned with the kanji, so you should be able to read this word no problem.
