## Mnemonics:
あの給食の後、三年生の学年の三分の一が吐いた。
* One third of the third grade threw up after school lunch.
* Your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> study</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> year</span> is kind of like the years you've studied. Where do you study? School. What is a year of study in a school? It's your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> school grade</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
