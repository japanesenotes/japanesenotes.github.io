## Mnemonics:

* 
* An <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> expert</span> who <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> watches over</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> defend</span>s people at all hours is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> nurse</span>. That's what nurses do, 24/7. They have to check up on people, defend their health, and watch over them, making sure they're okay. What a tough job.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
