## Mnemonics:
セール初日、モールは人でごった返しになり、まるで大乱戦のようだった。
* On the first day of the sale, the mall became super crowded, like it was some kind of battle royal.
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> riot</span> of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> war</span> is where things go nuts. This is a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> melee</span>! A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> free for all</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 乱, 戦
* Riot, War
