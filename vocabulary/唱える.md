## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> chant</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to chant</span>.
* Really, when you are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to chant</span>, the most important thing to do is to figure out your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> tone</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [とな](https://jisho.org/search/とな)</span>). If you get the tone down, the rest  comes naturally.
