| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> encourage</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to encourage someone</span> (or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to encourage</span>).
* Think about encouraging a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hag</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はげ](https://jisho.org/search/はげ)</span>). She needs encouragement because she's a hag, and nobody likes her much because of that.
