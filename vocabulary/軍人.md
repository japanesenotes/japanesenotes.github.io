## Mnemonics:
昨日、上司に俺はリストラで首になるって言われちゃってさ。だから、今、軍人になることを真剣に考えてるんだ。
* My boss told me yesterday that I am going to be fired, so now I’m seriously thinking about becoming a soldier.
* An <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> army</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> person</span> is someone in the army. What are those people called? They're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> soldier</span>s.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
