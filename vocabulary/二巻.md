## Mnemonics:

* 
* The number <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> two</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> scroll</span> is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> second volume</span>. With Japanese books, they often come in pieces with groups of three being common. This represents the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> second volume</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 二, 巻
* Two, Scroll
