## Mnemonics:

* 
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> year</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> congratulation</span>s <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> condition</span> is when you tell the world about your condition for the new year. What's a better way to tell them than by using a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> New Year's Card</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
