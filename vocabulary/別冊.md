## Mnemonics:
1985年、雑誌Tofuguの別冊本に、生まれたばかりのコウイチの写真が掲載されました。
* In 1985, newborn Koichi’s photograph was published in an extra issue of the Tofugu Magazine.
* Usually you call <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> books</span> volumes when they come in sets, right? This is like that, but <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> separate</span>, making this a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> separate volume</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [べつ](https://jisho.org/search/べつ)</span> gets shortened to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [べっ](https://jisho.org/search/べっ)</span>, though, probably because it's a <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つ](https://jisho.org/search/つ)</span> and that's what <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つ](https://jisho.org/search/つ)</span>s do for a living in jukugo words.
* 別, 冊
* Separate, Book Counter
