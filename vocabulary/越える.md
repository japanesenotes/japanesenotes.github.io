## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> go beyond</span>, the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to go beyond</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to go beyond</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> child</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [こ](https://jisho.org/search/こ)</span>) years in order to become an adult, you have to be 18 years old in the USA, and 20 years old in Japan.
