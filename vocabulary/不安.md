# <bigfont> 不安</bigfont> - Uneasiness, Anxiety, Unease 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 不安 | Uneasiness, Anxiety, Unease | ふあん |  [不](../kanjis/不.md) (Not), [安](../kanjis/安.md) (Relax, Cheap) | [Level 6](../levels/wk_level6.md) |

Previous vocab: [安心](安心.md) Next vocab: [平安](平安.md) 

## Mnemonics:
あの殺人事件を目撃してからというもの、不安を取り除くことができない。
* Since I witnessed the murder, I haven't been able to rid myself of these feelings of anxiety.
* When you're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> not</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> relax</span>ed how do you feel? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Uneasiness</span> is what you'd feel.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 不, 安
* Not, Relax
