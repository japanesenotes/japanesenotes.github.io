| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 怒気 | Anger, Fit Of Anger | どき |  [怒](../kanjis/怒.md) (Angry, Anger), [気](../kanjis/気.md) (Energy, Spirit) | [Level 29](../levels/wk_level29.md) |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* An <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> angry</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> energy</span> comes out of you when you have <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> anger</span>. It also can refer to a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> fit of anger</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 怒, 気
* Angry, Energy
