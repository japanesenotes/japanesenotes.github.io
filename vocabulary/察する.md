# <bigfont> 察する</bigfont> - To Guess, To Sense, To Presume 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [外交官](外交官.md) Next vocab: [察知](察知.md) 

## Mnemonics:
いくつかの記事を読んで、私はTofuguチームには何か暗黙の構想があることを察しました。
* From reading a few articles, I sense that the Tofugu team has some kind of implicit plan.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> guess</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to guess</span>. Alternatively, you can learn this as <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to sense</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to presume</span>.
* The reading for this is actually the same as what you learned with the kanji, but the <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [さつ](https://jisho.org/search/さつ)</span> is shortened to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [さっ](https://jisho.org/search/さっ)</span>. If you had <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to guess</span> be sure to guess with the shorter <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つ](https://jisho.org/search/つ)</span>.
