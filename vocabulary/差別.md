## Mnemonics:

* 
* You make a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> distinction</span> between people and based off that you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> separate</span> the people. This is terrible! Why so much <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> discrimination</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
