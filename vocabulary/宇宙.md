## Mnemonics:
フグ、サーモンはあなたが復縁したいって知らないのよ。 もし知ったら、彼女も違うように感じるかもしれないわ。もしかしたら、他の魚と月を見に宇宙へなんて行かないかもしれないわよ。
* Fugu, Salmon doesn’t know that you wanna get back together. If she did, she might feel differently. She might not even go to space to see the moon with a different fish.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> midair</span> of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> outer space</span> is the space that's in outer space which is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> outer space</span>, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> space</span>, and the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> universe</span>!
