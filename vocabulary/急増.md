| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 急増 | Sudden Increase, Increase Suddenly | きゅうぞう |  [急](../kanjis/急.md) (Hurry), [増](../kanjis/増.md) (Increase) | [Level 21](../levels/wk_level21.md) |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* An <span style="background-color:#ffcccb">  increase</span> that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hurries</span> is an increase that's quick. How about a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sudden increase</span>?
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 急, 増
* Hurry, Increase
