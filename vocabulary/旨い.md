## Mnemonics:

* 
* This is a single kanji with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [い](https://jisho.org/search/い)</span> on the end, meaning you know it's probably an adjective. What's the adjective form of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> delicious</span>? It's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> delicious</span>. It also means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> skillful</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> clever</span> as well, because delicious things are skillfully delicious, right?
* What's especially delicious? <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Horse</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [うま](https://jisho.org/search/うま)</span>) is delicious. Mmm, that delicious horse.
