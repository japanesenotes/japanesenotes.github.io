## Mnemonics:
あいつの話には落ちが無いんだよな。
* His speech didn't have much of a conclusion.
* When you're talking or writing a paper, the part that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> falls</span> at the end is the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> conclusion</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You learned this reading already so you should be able to read it!
