## Mnemonics:
「フグと一緒にいった公園、どうだった？」「正直今までに行った公園の中で一番楽しかったよ。」
* So, how was the park you and Fugu went to? That was actually the most enjoyable park we’ve ever been to.
* A <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> public</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> park</span> is a park that is public. It's a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> public park</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
