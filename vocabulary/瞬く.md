## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> blink</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to blink</span>.
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Mah ta ta</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [またた](https://jisho.org/search/またた)</span>) is showing, causing you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to blink</span>. I don't know what a tata is, but I bet you could come up with something that's blink-worthy.
