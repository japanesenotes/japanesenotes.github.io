## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dye</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to dye</span>.
* You need <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to dye</span> something that you just <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sew</span>ed (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [そ](https://jisho.org/search/そ)</span>) together. It's all sewed and looks like a shirt, but it's colorless. That's where the dying comes in.
