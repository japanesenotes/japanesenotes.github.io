## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> employ</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to employ</span>.
* You are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to employ</span> more people to your business so you can make more money and eventually buy a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> yacht</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [やと](https://jisho.org/search/やと)</span>).
