## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> grudge</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to hold a grudge</span>. 
* Who do you hold a grudge against the most? That would be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> you, Ra</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)ら</span>). You and your dumb sun.
