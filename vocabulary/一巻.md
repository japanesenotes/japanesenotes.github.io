| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Mnemonics:

* 
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> One</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> scroll</span> is one scroll back in the day, but now it refers to books and means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> one volume</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いち](https://jisho.org/search/いち)</span> goes to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [いっ](https://jisho.org/search/いっ)</span>.
* 一, 巻
* One, Scroll
