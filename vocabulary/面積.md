| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 面積 | Area | めんせき |  [面](../kanjis/面.md) (Face, Surface), [積](../kanjis/積.md) (Accumulate) | [Level 29](../levels/wk_level29.md) |

Previous vocab: [積もる](積もる.md) Next vocab: [体積](体積.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> surface</span> that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> accumulate</span>s into one area is a particular <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> area</span>. This has to do with math. When you add all the surfaces together by accumulating them into one calculation, you get yourself the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> area</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 面, 積
* Face, Accumulate
