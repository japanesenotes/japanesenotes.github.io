## Mnemonics:
コウイチはトーフグの創始者であり、近代日本を代表する啓蒙思想家である。
* Koichi is the founder of Tofugu and one of the great modern Japanese thinkers.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> concept</span> you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> think</span> about is your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> thought</span>s. It is your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> idea</span>s.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 思, 想
