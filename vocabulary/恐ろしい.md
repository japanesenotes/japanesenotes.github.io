## Mnemonics:

* 
* This is a single kanji with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [い](https://jisho.org/search/い)</span> on the end, meaning you know it's probably an adjective. What's the adjective form of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> fear</span>? When you feel fear, you think something is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> scary</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: How <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> scary</span> is it? It's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Oh so</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [おそ](https://jisho.org/search/おそ)</span>) scary!
