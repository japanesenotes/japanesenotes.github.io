## Mnemonics:
ちょっと、コウイチ。落ち着いてよ。 親しい友人たちに誰か80セント貸してあげれる人がいないか聞いてみてあげるから。
* Oh Koichi. Calm down. I’ll ask my close friends if anyone can lend you the 80 cents.
* Your <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> parent</span>s are very close to you, most likely. They are your closest relatives. They are the ones who are <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> intimate</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> friendly</span> with your affairs. They want to know everything!
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: <br />Those who are most <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> intimate</span> with you hang out with you even when you're doing gross things, like sitting on the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> sh*tter</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [した](https://jisho.org/search/した)</span>). Gross, your family is.
