| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 自動的 | Automatic | じどうてき |  [自](../kanjis/自.md) (Self, Oneself), [動](../kanjis/動.md) (Move), [的](../kanjis/的.md) (Target, Al, ~Al, Like, ~Like) | [Level 16](../levels/wk_level16.md) |

Previous vocab: [自動](自動.md) Next vocab: [基本的](基本的.md) 

## Mnemonics:
もし誰かがあなたの家に侵入したら、この機械が自動的に警備会社に通報します。
* If someone breaks into your house this machine will automatically send a message to the security company.
* An <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> automatic</span><span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> al</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [自動](https://jisho.org/search/自動)</span> + <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [的](https://jisho.org/search/的)</span>) thing of course isn't automatical. A better way to say it is to cut off the -al and call it <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> automatic</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. It will help to know the reading of <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [自動](https://jisho.org/search/自動)</span> as well.
* 自, 動, 的
* Self, Move, Target
