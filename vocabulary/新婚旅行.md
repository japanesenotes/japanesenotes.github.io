## Mnemonics:

* 
* You know that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [旅行](https://jisho.org/search/旅行)</span> is a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> trip</span>. The other part is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> new</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> marriage</span>. What's the trip you go on after a new marriage? The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> honeymoon</span>!
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
