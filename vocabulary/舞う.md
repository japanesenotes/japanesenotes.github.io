## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> dance</span> and the verb vocab version means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to dance</span>, as well as <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to flutter</span>.
* Who is the greatest dancer of them all? China's previous ruler, <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Mao</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [まう](https://jisho.org/search/まう)</span>).
