## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> smash</span> and the verb vocab version of the kanji is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to smash</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to break</span>.
* You want <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to smash</span> my stuff? That's coo' <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [だ](https://jisho.org/search/だ)</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [くだ](https://jisho.org/search/くだ)</span>). Whatever you wanna do.
