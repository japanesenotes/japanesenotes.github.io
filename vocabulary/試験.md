# <bigfont> 試験</bigfont> - Examination, Test, Experiment, Exam 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 試験 | Examination, Test, Experiment, Exam | しけん |  [試](../kanjis/試.md) (Try, Attempt), [験](../kanjis/験.md) (Test) | [Level 9](../levels/wk_level9.md) |

Previous vocab: [実験](実験.md) Next vocab: [乱れる](乱れる.md) 

## Mnemonics:
「ところで、試験はどうだった？」「パパ、その話はやめてよ。」
* By the way, how was the exam? Stop talking about that, dad.
* If you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> attempt</span> a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> test</span>, you're taking an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> examination</span>. There are a lot of these in Japan, so make sure you know this word if you want to fit in with all the whining and whinging that students do, especially come the final year of high school.
* This is a jukugo word that uses the on'yomi readings of the kanji. You should be able to read this on your own.
* 試, 験
* Try, Test
