# <bigfont> 人気</bigfont> - Popular, Popularity 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 人気 | Popular, Popularity | にんき |  [人](../kanjis/人.md) (Person), [気](../kanjis/気.md) (Energy, Spirit) | [Level 4](../levels/wk_level4.md) |

Previous vocab: [気分](気分.md) Next vocab: [元気](元気.md) 

## Mnemonics:
私がこの辺りで一番人気の高いベリー採集者であります。」と熊が言った。
* I am the most popular berry collector around here, said the bear.
* If a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> person</span> has a lot of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> energy</span> then they're very charismatic and fun! What's another feature of charismatic / fun people? They are most likely <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> popular</span> as well.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 人, 気
* Person, Energy
