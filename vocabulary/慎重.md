## Mnemonics:

* 
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Humility</span> is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> heavy</span>, so it's best nobody knows your secret. You should be <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> discreet</span> instead.
* This is a jukugo word that uses the on'yomi readings of the kanji. You just need to remember the alternative reading for <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [重](https://jisho.org/search/重)</span>. If you aren't discreet or careful, the evil Mrs. <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Chou</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ちょう](https://jisho.org/search/ちょう)</span>)will find you and kill you!
* 慎, 重
* Humility, Heavy
