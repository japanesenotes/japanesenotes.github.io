## Mnemonics:
どうして頭に蛭を付けているんですか？
* Why are you attaching leeches to your head?
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> attach</span> so the verb version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to attach</span>.
* The reading is actually the same as the one you learned with the kanji, meaning you should be able to read this one just fine.
