## Mnemonics:

* 
* When you <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> lack</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> stretch</span>ing, your body has to get that energy out somehow, so you end up <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> yawning</span>.
* The reading for this is an exception. Here's a mnemonic to help you:<br />You've never seen more <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> yawning</span> than when you performed <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> acu</span>puncture (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [あく](https://jisho.org/search/あく)</span>) on a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> bee</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [び](https://jisho.org/search/び)</span>).
