# <bigfont> 断る</bigfont> - To Refuse, To Reject 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 断る | To Refuse, To Reject | ことわる |  [断](../kanjis/断.md) (Cut Off) | [Level 21](../levels/wk_level21.md) |

Previous vocab: [素材](素材.md) Next vocab: [判断](判断.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cut off</span>. What happens when you do the action of cutting someone off? You're going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to refuse</span> them of whatever it is they want.
* By trying <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to refuse</span> this person, you're going to start a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [事](https://jisho.org/search/事)</span> war</span> (a war of actions). They will do one war-like action, then you will do a war-like action until it escalates into a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [事](https://jisho.org/search/事)</span> war</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ことわる](https://jisho.org/search/ことわる)</span>).
* 断
* Cut Off
