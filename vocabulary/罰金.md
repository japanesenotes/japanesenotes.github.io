# <bigfont> 罰金</bigfont> - A Fine, Fine 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 罰金 | A Fine, Fine | ばっきん |  [罰](../kanjis/罰.md) (Penalty, Punishment), [金](../kanjis/金.md) (Gold) | [Level 22](../levels/wk_level22.md) |

Previous vocab: [罰](罰.md) Next vocab: [罰ゲーム](罰ゲーム.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> Penalty</span> <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> gold</span> is the money you pay when you get a penalty. It's for <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> a fine</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ばつ](https://jisho.org/search/ばつ)</span> gets changed to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ばっ](https://jisho.org/search/ばっ)</span> though, so don't get it wrong (or you'll get a <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [罰金](https://jisho.org/search/罰金)</span>!).
* 罰, 金
* Penalty, Gold
