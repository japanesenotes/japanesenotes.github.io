## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> overnight</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to stay the night</span>.
* We are just going to stay <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to</span>night (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [と](https://jisho.org/search/と)</span>) and maybe <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to</span>morrow (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [と](https://jisho.org/search/と)</span>) tonight.
