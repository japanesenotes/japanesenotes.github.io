## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> exhaust</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to exhaust</span>.
* If something is exhausted it's because you used up <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> too</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [つ](https://jisho.org/search/つ)</span>) much of it.
