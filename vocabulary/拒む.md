## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> refuse</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to refuse</span>.
* What are you refusing? You are refusing a child, because children are all bad.
