# <bigfont> 不人気</bigfont> - Unpopular, Not Popular 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 不人気 | Unpopular, Not Popular | ふにんき |  [不](../kanjis/不.md) (Not), [人](../kanjis/人.md) (Person), [気](../kanjis/気.md) (Energy, Spirit) | [Level 7](../levels/wk_level7.md) |

Previous vocab: [中学生](中学生.md) Next vocab: [見直す](見直す.md) 

## Mnemonics:
「お前の母ちゃんに言ってやる」が口癖だったため、彼はクラスメートに不人気だった。
* He was unpopular among his classmates because his favorite phrase was, I’m gonna tell your mom.
* You already know <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [人気](https://jisho.org/search/人気)</span> which means popular. And you also know that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [不](https://jisho.org/search/不)</span> means not. So what do you get when you combine the two? Something that's <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> not popular</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> unpopular</span>.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
* 不, 人, 気
* Not, Person, Energy
