# <bigfont> 思い出す</bigfont> - To Remember, To Recall 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 思い出す | To Remember, To Recall | おもいだす |  [思](../kanjis/思.md) (Think, Thought), [出](../kanjis/出.md) (Exit) | [Level 8](../levels/wk_level8.md) |

Previous vocab: [近づく](近づく.md) Next vocab: [大学生](大学生.md) 

## Mnemonics:
人に思い出してもらう一番の方法は、その人たちからお金を借りることだ。
* The best way to make somebody remember you is to borrow money from them.
* You know that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [思う](https://jisho.org/search/思う)</span> means to think (so <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [思い](https://jisho.org/search/思い)</span> is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> a thought</span>). You also know that <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [出す](https://jisho.org/search/出す)</span> means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to remove</span>. So, when you remove a thought, you are remembering something. This is a verb, so the verb form of remembering something is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to remember</span>.
* The reading for this word is the kun'yomi readings. You should know the words <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [思う](https://jisho.org/search/思う)</span> and <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [出す](https://jisho.org/search/出す)</span> and their readings, so you should be able to read this word if you know those words.
* 思, 出
