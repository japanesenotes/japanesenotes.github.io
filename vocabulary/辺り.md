# <bigfont> 辺り</bigfont> - Area, Vicinity, Neighborhood 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 辺り | Area, Vicinity, Neighborhood | あたり |  [辺](../kanjis/辺.md) (Area) | [Level 7](../levels/wk_level7.md) |

Previous vocab: [国民](国民.md) Next vocab: [この辺](この辺.md) 

## Mnemonics:
彼のトイレはこの辺りに違いない。
* His bathroom must be somewhere around here.
* This is the noun version of the kanji for <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> area</span>. This makes it have the same meaning as the kanji: <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> area</span> or <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> vicinity</span>.
* Since this word consists of a kanji with hiragana attached, you can bet that it will use the kun'yomi reading. You didn't learn that reading with this kanji, so here's a mnemonic to help you: <br />If you think about an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> area</span> that you're in, it's the place you're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> at</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [あた](https://jisho.org/search/あた)</span>).
