## Mnemonics:
もし自分の土地があれば、ようやくそこに自分のベッドルームを手に入れることができるぜ！
* If I had some land of my own, I’d finally get my own bedroom.
* The <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> soil</span> of the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> earth</span> is the portion of the earth that is made up of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> land</span>.
* The reading is the on'yomi reading, but <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [土](https://jisho.org/search/土)</span> gets modified to <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [と](https://jisho.org/search/と)</span> for some reason. Just remember that the land you're standing on is made of <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> toe</span>s... millions of them. Yuck.
