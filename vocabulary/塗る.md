## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> paint</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to paint</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to plaster</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to lacquer</span>.
* Let's put a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> new</span> (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぬ](https://jisho.org/search/ぬ)</span>) layer of paint on the wall. Then we'll plaster it.
