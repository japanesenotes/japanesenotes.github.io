## Mnemonics:

* 
* You <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> examine</span> then <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> search</span>. These are the steps you take in order to perform a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> lookup</span> of some kind (or, as the case may be in modern times, do an <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> internet search</span>).
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well.
