# <bigfont> 滅ぼす</bigfont> - To Destroy, To Annihilate 

| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 滅ぼす | To Destroy, To Annihilate | ほろぼす |  [滅](../kanjis/滅.md) (Destroy) | [Level 43](../levels/wk_level43.md) |

Previous vocab: [安泰](安泰.md) Next vocab: [滅亡](滅亡.md) 

## Mnemonics:

* 
* Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> destroy</span> and the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to destroy</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to annihilate</span>.
* You are going <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to destroy</span> the <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> hoe roe</span>. This is the roe that comes from the hoe tools. If they create too much roe, they will create too many hoes. And if there are too many hoes, bad things will happen.
* 滅
* Destroy
