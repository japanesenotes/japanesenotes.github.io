| Vocabulary | Meanings | Reading | Characters | Level |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Previous vocab: [生存](生存.md) Next vocab: [留守](留守.md) 

## Mnemonics:
いいか、わが愛犬よ、よく聞け。家族を守るのは、お前の責任だ。
* Listen to me, my dog. It’s your responsibility to defend your family.
* This word consists of kanji with hiragana attached. Because the hiragana ends with an <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [う](https://jisho.org/search/う)</span> sound, you know this word is a verb. The kanji itself means <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> protect</span> so the verb vocab version is <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to protect</span> and also <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to defend</span>.
* You need <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> to protect</span> against breast cancer, so you get yourself a <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> mammo</span>gram (<span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [まも](https://jisho.org/search/まも)</span>).
