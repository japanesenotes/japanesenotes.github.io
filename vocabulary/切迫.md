## Mnemonics:

* 
* We're <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> cut</span> right down to the end, reaching that <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> urge</span> position where <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> urgency</span> and <span style="background-color:#ffcccb"> pressure</span> are so strong.
* This is a jukugo word, which usually means on'yomi readings from the kanji. If you know the readings of your kanji you'll know how to read this as well. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [せつ](https://jisho.org/search/せつ)</span> of <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [切](https://jisho.org/search/切)</span> gets shortened to a small <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [っ](https://jisho.org/search/っ)</span>. The <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [はく](https://jisho.org/search/はく)</span> becomes <span style="background-color:#fed8b1"> [ぱく](https://jisho.org/search/ぱく)</span>.
